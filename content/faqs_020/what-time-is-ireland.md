<h2>Time Zone in Ireland</h2>

Ireland is located in the Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) zone. This is the same time zone as the United Kingdom, so when it’s 12:00 p.m. in London, it’s also 12:00 p.m. in Ireland.

<h2>Daylight Savings Time in Ireland</h2>

Daylight Savings Time (DST) is observed in Ireland from the last Sunday of March until the last Sunday of October. During this period, the clocks are moved forward one hour, making it one hour ahead of GMT. This means that when it’s 1:00 p.m. in London, it’s 2:00 p.m. in Ireland.
