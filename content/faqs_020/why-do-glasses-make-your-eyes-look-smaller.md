<h2>What Causes Your Eyes to Look Smaller in Glasses?</h2>

There are several factors that may cause your eyes to look smaller in glasses. The most common cause is the frame of the glasses. The frame can obstruct your eyes, making them appear to be smaller than they actually are. This can be especially noticeable if the frame is thick or bulky. Additionally, if the frame is too wide, it may cover more of your face, causing your eyes to look smaller.

Another factor is the lens itself. If the lens is too large, it can cover up a large portion of your eyes, making them appear smaller. Additionally, if the lens is too close to your eyes, it can cause the eyes to look smaller than they actually are.
