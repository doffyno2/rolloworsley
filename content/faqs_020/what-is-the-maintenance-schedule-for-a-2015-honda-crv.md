<h2>What Does the Maintenance Schedule for a 2015 Honda CRV Include?</h2>

<p>The maintenance schedule for a 2015 Honda CRV is divided into two categories: regular maintenance and periodic maintenance. Regular maintenance includes things such as oil changes, tire rotations, checking and replacing fluids, and inspecting the brakes. Periodic maintenance includes things such as replacing the spark plugs, changing the air filter, and inspecting the belts and hoses. It is important to follow both regular and periodic maintenance schedules if you want to keep your Honda CRV in good condition.</p>
