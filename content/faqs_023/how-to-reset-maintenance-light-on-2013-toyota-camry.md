<p>The maintenance light on your 2013 Toyota Camry is a helpful tool that lets you know when it's time for your vehicle to receive regular maintenance. The maintenance light can be reset when the maintenance is complete, so you can keep track of when it's time for another service.</p>

<h2>What is the Maintenance Light?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light is a small dashboard indicator that looks like a wrench. It will illuminate when it's time for your vehicle to receive regular maintenance. This light is specific to your Toyota Camry, and it may look slightly different depending on the year, trim, and model of your vehicle. </p>

<h2>What Maintenance Does the Maintenance Light Indicate?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light indicates that it's time for your Toyota Camry to receive regular maintenance. This may include an oil change, tire rotation, spark plug replacement, and other necessary services. It's important to follow the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule to ensure your vehicle is running optimally and safely.</p>

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2013 Toyota Camry</h2>

<p>The maintenance light can be reset on a 2013 Toyota Camry by following these simple steps:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Turn on the Ignition</h3>

<p>The first step is to turn on the ignition of the Camry. This will ensure that the light is illuminated and able to be reset.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Locate the Maintenance Light</h3>

<p>Once the ignition is on, locate the maintenance light on the dashboard. It will be a small icon that looks like a wrench. </p>

<h3>Step 3: Press and Hold the Trip Reset Button</h3>

<p>Press and hold the trip reset button located on the right side of the steering wheel. Keep pressing and holding the button until the maintenance light turns off.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Turn Off the Ignition</h3>

<p>Once the maintenance light is off, turn off the ignition to complete the reset process. </p>
