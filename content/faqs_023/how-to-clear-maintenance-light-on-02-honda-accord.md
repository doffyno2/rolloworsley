<h2>What Does the Maintenance Light Indicate?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light is a warning indicator that is designed to alert you when your 02 Honda Accord needs to be serviced. The maintenance light can be illuminated for a variety of reasons, such as an oil change, air filter replacement, or spark plug replacement. It's important to note that the maintenance light doesn't indicate an immediate danger, but rather that your vehicle should be serviced as soon as possible.</p>

<h2>How to Clear the Maintenance Light on 02 Honda Accord</h2>

<p>Clearing the maintenance light on your 02 Honda Accord is relatively straightforward, and can be done in just a few steps. Firstly, ensure that your vehicle is off and the key is out of the ignition. Then, locate the maintenance light, which is typically located on the dashboard of your vehicle. Once you have located the maintenance light, press and hold the reset button for approximately 5 seconds. The maintenance light should now be cleared and you can start your vehicle as normal.</p>
