<h2>Introduction</h2>
Having a backpack that smells like smoke can be a real nuisance. Not only will it cause people to give you strange looks, but it can also be embarrassing. <h2>Vinegar</h2>
Vinegar is a common household item that can be used to remove smoke smell from a North Face backpack. Fill a bowl with vinegar and place it inside the backpack. Let it sit for a few hours, then take it out and air out the backpack. The vinegar will absorb the smoke smell and leave your backpack smelling fresh and clean.
