<h2>Step-by-Step Instructions</h2>

<p>Adjusting the seat on your Husqvarna riding mower is a simple process that can be completed in a few minutes. Follow the steps below to make sure you adjust the seat correctly:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Refer to the instructions that came with your mower for the exact steps for adjusting the seat.</li>
  <li>If your mower does not have the instructions, locate the adjustment screws or levers on the seat. These can usually be found on the sides of the seat.</li>
  <li>Using your Phillips head screwdriver, loosen the screws or levers. This will allow you to adjust the height and position of the seat.</li>
  <li>Using your adjustable wrench, adjust the screws or levers until you have the desired height and position.</li>
  <li>Once you have the seat adjusted to your liking, tighten the screws or levers with the Phillips head screwdriver.</li>
</ol>
