<h2>Step 1: Unplug the Ice Maker</h2>

<p>The first step to resetting your Igloo ice maker is to unplug it from the power source. This will turn the machine off and reset it. Once you’ve unplugged the machine, wait a few seconds to allow it to reset.</p>

<p>Once the ice maker has been unplugged, turn it off by rotating the power switch located on the back of the machine. This will turn the ice maker off and reset it.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Turn the Ice Maker Back On</h2>

<p>After you’ve unplugged and turned off the ice maker, it’s time to turn it back on. To do this, plug the ice maker back into the power source and then turn the power switch back on. You should hear the ice maker start to run again.</p>

<p>Once the ice maker is running again, you’ll need to adjust the water level. To do this, turn the water level adjuster located on the back of the machine. This will allow you to adjust the water level so that the ice maker can make enough ice.</p>
