How to Make a Boonie Hat Look Cool


Boonie hats are a popular style of hat that are perfect for the summertime. They offer protection from the sun’s UV rays and also look fashionable. When looking for a new boonie hat, one of the first things you should consider is the color and pattern. For a classic look, opt for a solid color or a simple print. One of the best ways to make your boonie hat look cool is to accessorize it. A few simple accessories can take your look from plain to stylish in no time. Finally, the best way to make your boonie hat look cool is to pair it with the right outfit. Choose clothing items that will complement the color and pattern of your hat.
