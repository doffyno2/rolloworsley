<p>The maintenance light on the dashboard of the 2017 Honda Pilot is an indicator that it's time for the vehicle to be serviced. <h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light</h2>

<p>The process of resetting the maintenance light in the 2017 Honda Pilot is relatively simple. To begin, make sure your Pilot is turned off and the keys are removed from the ignition. Next, locate the small button labeled "Reset" located on the dashboard. Press and hold the reset button for three seconds until the maintenance light turns off. </p>
