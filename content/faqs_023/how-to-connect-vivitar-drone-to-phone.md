Vivitar drones are some of the most popular consumer drones on the market today. They offer an impressive range of features and specs, making them an attractive choice for amateur and professional drone pilots alike. But like any other drone, flying and controlling it requires the user to connect it to their phone. This tutorial will show you how to connect a Vivitar drone to your phone so that you can enjoy the full benefit of its features.

<h2>What You Need to Connect Vivitar Drone to Phone</h2>

Before you can connect your Vivitar drone to your phone, you'll need a few things. The most important is the drone itself. You'll also need a compatible smartphone with a working Wi-Fi connection, and you'll need the Vivitar drone remote control app. This app is available for both Android and iOS smartphones and can be downloaded from the respective app stores. Once you have all of these things, you're ready to get started.

<h2>Steps to Connect Vivitar Drone to Phone</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Turn on the Drone</h3>

The first step to connecting your Vivitar drone to your phone is to turn on the drone. To do this, press and hold the power button on the drone until it powers on. Once it's on, the lights on the front of the drone should turn blue.

<h3>Step 2: Connect the Drone to Your Phone</h3>

The next step is to connect the drone to your phone. To do this, open the Vivitar drone app on your smartphone and select the “Connect” button at the top of the screen. Once you’ve done this, the app will search for any nearby drones. When it finds your Vivitar drone, it will display its name in the app. Select the name of your drone and press the “Connect” button.

<h3>Step 3: Configure the Settings</h3>

Once the Vivitar drone is connected to your phone, you can configure the settings. In the app, you can adjust the drone's speed, altitude, and other settings. You can also set the camera resolution and other video settings. Once you’re satisfied with the settings, you’re ready to fly!

<h3>Step 4: Fly the Drone</h3>

Now that your Vivitar drone is connected to your phone, you’re ready to fly it. To do this, open the Vivitar drone app and select the “Fly” button at the bottom of the screen. This will launch the drone’s controls, allowing you to take off and start flying.
