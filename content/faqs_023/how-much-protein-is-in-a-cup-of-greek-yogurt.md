# How Much Protein Is In A Cup Of Greek Yogurt?

Greek yogurt is a popular dairy product that is enjoyed by many people around the world. It is a great source of protein, making it a favorite among health-conscious individuals. But how much protein is in a cup of Greek yogurt? This article will answer that question and provide additional information about the nutritional content of Greek yogurt.

## What Is Greek Yogurt?

Greek yogurt is a type of strained yogurt that is made with milk and thickened with a thickening agent such as gelatin or pectin. This type of yogurt is high in fat, but it also contains a good amount of protein and calcium. Greek yogurt is a popular choice among people who are looking for a healthy snack or meal replacement.

## How Much Protein Is In A Cup Of Greek Yogurt?

A cup of Greek yogurt contains approximately 20 grams of protein. This is a significant amount of protein, especially when compared to other dairy products such as milk or cheese. Greek yogurt also contains other essential nutrients such as calcium and vitamin B12.

## What Are The Benefits Of Eating Greek Yogurt?

Greek yogurt is a great source of protein, which can help to build and maintain muscle mass. It is also a good source of calcium, which helps to promote strong bones and teeth. Greek yogurt also contains probiotics, which are beneficial bacteria that help to maintain a healthy digestive system.

## How Can I Add Greek Yogurt To My Diet?

Greek yogurt can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. It can be enjoyed plain or flavored with fruit or honey. Greek yogurt can also be used in recipes such as smoothies, sauces, and dips. Greek yogurt can also be enjoyed as a snack on its own or as a topping for salads, oatmeal, or toast.
