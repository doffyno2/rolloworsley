<p>Maintaining your 2012 Camry Hybrid is essential to keep it running smoothly and efficiently. One way to keep your car in tip-top shape is to regularly reset the maintenance light, which indicates when it’s time for an oil change or other routine maintenance. Resetting the maintenance light is a simple process, and can be done in just a few steps.</p>

<h2>Steps to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2012 Camry Hybrid</h2>

<p>Before you begin the reset process, make sure your vehicle is turned off and the key is in the “off” position. </p>

<ul>
  <li>Step 1: Locate the fuse box located under the dashboard on the driver side of your 2012 Camry Hybrid.</li>
  <li>Step 2: Locate the reset switch. It should be labeled “Reset” or “Maintenance”.</li>
  <li>Step 3: Push the reset switch. This will reset the maintenance light.</li>
  <li>Step 4: Turn your key to the “on” position and start the car. The maintenance light should now be off.</li>
</ul>

<p>If the maintenance light does not turn off after resetting, you may need to take your vehicle to a mechanic or dealership for further inspection. </p>
