<h2>Step One: Check the Battery</h2>
The first step when charging a skilling backpack is to check the battery. Most backpacks will have either a rechargeable or disposable battery. If it is a rechargeable battery, you will need to connect it to an outlet or USB port. If it is a disposable battery, you will need to replace it with a new one.

<h2>Step Two: Connect to Power Source</h2>

<h2>Step Four: Charge the Battery</h2>

<h2>Step Five: Unplug the Charger</h2>
When the battery is fully charged, you will need to unplug the charger from the backpack.

<h2>Step Six: Test the Battery</h2>
