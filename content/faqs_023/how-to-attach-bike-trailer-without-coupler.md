<h2>What is a Coupler?</h2>

<p>A coupler is a mechanism which is used to attach the bike trailer to your bicycle. It is usually made of metal and attaches to the back of your bicycle, allowing the trailer to connect to it. The most common type of coupler is the hitch-style, which has two metal arms that slide into the trailer's frame. Other types of couplers, such as the quick-release coupler, are also available.</p>
