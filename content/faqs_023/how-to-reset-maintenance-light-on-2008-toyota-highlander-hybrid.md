<h2>Steps to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2008 Toyota Highlander Hybrid</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Turn the Ignition On</h3>

<h3>Step 2: Locate the Maintenance Light</h3>

<p>Once the ignition is on, you will be able to locate the maintenance light on the instrument panel. It should be located near the speedometer. </p>

<h3>Step 3: Push and Hold the Reset Button</h3>

<p>Once you have located the maintenance light, you must find the reset button. It should be located near the light itself. Push and hold the button until the light turns off. This should take a few seconds. </p>

<h3>Step 4: Turn the Ignition Off</h3>

<p>Once the light has been reset, you must turn the ignition off. This will ensure that the reset has been successful. </p>
