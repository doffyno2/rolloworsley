<h3>Step 1: Soften the Adhesive</h3>
<p>The first step in removing 3M VHB double sided tape is to soften the adhesive. You can do this by applying rubbing alcohol or lighter fluid to the area. Allow it to soak for several minutes, then use a plastic putty knife to gently scrape away the softened adhesive.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Heat the Adhesive</h3>
<p>The next step is to heat the adhesive. You can do this using a heat gun or a hair dryer. Aim the heat gun or hair dryer at the adhesive for several minutes, then use a plastic putty knife to gently scrape away the softened adhesive.</p>
