In order to reset the Maintenance Required light, you'll need to perform a few simple steps.</p>

<h2>Step 1: Locate the Maintenance Required Light</h2>

<p>The Maintenance Required light is located on the dashboard of the 2008 Toyota Tundra. It is a yellow or amber light shaped like a wrench. When the light is illuminated, it indicates that your truck needs service.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Turn off the Ignition</h2>

<p>Once you have located the Maintenance Required light, you'll need to turn off the ignition. To do this, turn the key to the "Off" position.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Wait 10 Seconds</h2>

<p>Once the ignition is off, you'll need to wait 10 seconds. This will reset the Maintenance Required light.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Turn the Ignition On</h2>

<p>After waiting 10 seconds, turn the key to the "On" position. This will turn the Maintenance Required light back on.</p>

<h2>Step 5: Turn the Ignition Off Again</h2>

<p>Once the Maintenance Required light is back on, you'll need to turn the key to the "Off" position again. This will reset the light.</p>

<h2>Step 6: Turn the Ignition On Again</h2>

<p>Once the Maintenance Required light is off, you can turn the key back to the "On" position. The Maintenance Required light should now be reset.</p>
