<h2>How to Measure Your Child’s Head</h2>

<p>The first step in determining the right helmet size for your 7-year-old is to measure his or her head. To do this, you’ll need a tailor’s measuring tape. Start by wrapping the measuring tape around the circumference of your child’s head, positioning it just above the eyebrows. Make sure the measuring tape is snug, but not too tight. Once you have the measurement, you can then compare it to the size chart provided by the helmet manufacturer.</p>
