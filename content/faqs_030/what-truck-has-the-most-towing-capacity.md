<h2>What Is Towing Capacity?</h2>

Before we dive into which trucks have the most towing capacity, let's take a moment to explain what towing capacity actually is. To put it simply, towing capacity is the maximum weight that a vehicle can safely tow. It is important to understand this concept because it can help you determine which truck is best for your needs.

<h2>How Is Towing Capacity Measured?</h2>

The towing capacity of a truck is usually measured in pounds. This means that the higher the number, the more weight the truck can safely tow. It is important to remember that the towing capacity of a vehicle is not the same as the payload capacity. Payload capacity is the maximum weight that a vehicle can safely carry in its bed or cabin.
