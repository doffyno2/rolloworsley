<h2>What to Wear</h2>

<p>The US Army requires that all soldiers wear the appropriate uniform for the occasion when attending boot camp graduation. For males, this consists of Class A Greens or Dress Blues, an Army service cap, and black dress shoes. Females are required to wear the Class A Greens, an Army service cap, a white blouse, and black dress shoes. The dress uniform should be clean and pressed, and the shoes should be polished. </p>

<p>Along with the dress uniform, soldiers may also wear a few accessories. For males, this includes a white belt, a black tie, and black gloves. Females may wear a black skirt, a white belt, a black tie, and black gloves. Soldiers may also wear medals and awards that they have earned. </p>

<h2>Inspections</h2>

<p>Before they are allowed to attend boot camp graduation, soldiers must pass an inspection. During the inspection, soldiers are expected to be in the appropriate uniform and have a neat appearance. Soldiers who fail the inspection will not be allowed to participate in the graduation ceremony. </p>

<h2>Other Guidelines</h2>

<p>In addition to the uniform requirements, there are a few other guidelines that soldiers must follow for boot camp graduation.
