<h2>What is a Beach Wagon?</h2> 

A beach wagon is a type of wagon designed to make it easier to transport items to and from the beach. Beach wagons usually have large wheels that make it easy to move them over sand or other uneven terrain. They are usually collapsible, so they can be easily stored when not in use. Some beach wagons come with a canopy and other features that make it easy to carry items to and from the beach.
