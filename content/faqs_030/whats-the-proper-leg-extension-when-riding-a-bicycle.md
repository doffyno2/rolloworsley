<h2>What is the Proper Leg Extension When Riding a Bicycle?</h2>

The proper leg extension when riding a bicycle is when your legs are straight and your feet are flat on the pedals. This position is important for several reasons. First, it helps to keep your legs in an optimal position for pedaling. When your legs are extended, your muscles are able to work more efficiently, which allows you to pedal more effectively. Secondly, having your legs extended helps to keep your center of gravity low, which helps to make your ride more stable and less likely to tip over. Lastly, having your legs extended while riding a bicycle helps to keep your body in an upright position, which helps to improve your posture and your overall comfort while riding.

<h3>2. Adjust Your Pedals</h3>

The next step to achieving the proper leg extension when riding a bicycle is to adjust your pedals. If your pedals are too far away from your seat, your legs won't be able to extend fully.
