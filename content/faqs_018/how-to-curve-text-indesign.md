<h2>What is Curved Text in InDesign?</h2>

<p>Curved text in InDesign is a feature that allows you to bend or curl your text around a shape or path. It is a great way to create eye-catching designs that will stand out from the crowd. You can also use it to create interesting and creative layouts for your projects.</p>

<h2>How to Curve Text in InDesign</h2>

<p>Curving text in InDesign is actually quite simple. First, you'll need to create a path or shape that you want to curve your text around – this could be a circle, an oval, or any other shape you want to use. Once you’ve created your path or shape, select the Type Tool (T) and click on the path or shape. This will open the Text on Path Options window. From here, you can choose whether to align the text to the inside or outside of the path, and also adjust the spacing and alignment of the text.</p>

<p>Next, you can type your text into the text box that appears. This text will now be curved around the path or shape you have created. You can also adjust the font size and style of the text, as well as the spacing between the characters. Once you’re happy with the results, you can click the Apply button to apply the curved text to your design.</p>
