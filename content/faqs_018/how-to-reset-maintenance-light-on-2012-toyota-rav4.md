<p>The maintenance light on your Toyota RAV4 is designed to let you know when it’s time for a tune-up. <h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2012 Toyota RAV4</h2>

<p>Resetting the maintenance light on your Toyota RAV4 is a simple process. First, you need to locate the “Trip” button on your dashboard. This button is usually located near the speedometer and fuel gauge. Once you’ve located the “Trip” button, press and hold it for a few seconds. You should hear a “beep” sound. This should reset the maintenance light on your dashboard.</p>
