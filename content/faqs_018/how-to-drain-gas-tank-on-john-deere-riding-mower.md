<h2>Step 1: Shut Off the Mower</h2>

<p>The first step to draining the gas tank on a John Deere riding mower is to shut off the mower. This is done by turning the ignition key to the "off" position. Once the mower is shut off, the gas tank can be drained.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Disconnect the Gas Tank</h2>

<p>The next step is to disconnect the gas tank from the mower. To do this, locate the gas tank and unscrew the bolts that connect it to the mower. Once the bolts are removed, the gas tank can be disconnected from the mower.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Drain the Gas Tank</h2>

<p>Once the gas tank is disconnected from the mower, it can be drained. To do this, place the tank on a stable surface and place a pan or bucket underneath it. Open the drain valve and allow the gas to drain out of the tank. Once the gas has drained, the tank can be reattached to the mower.</p>
