<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light</h2>

To reset the maintenance light on your Corolla 2015, you will first need to locate the “check engine” light. It is typically located on the dashboard, either just above the glove box or near the steering column. Once you have located it, you can push and hold the “check engine” button until the light turns off. This should reset the maintenance light and get it back up and running.
