How to Reset Maintenance Light on 2007 Toyota Camry XLE

<h2>What is the Maintenance Light?</h2>
The maintenance light, also known as the service engine soon light, is a small light in the instrument cluster of the 2007 Toyota Camry XLE that indicates the car needs service or maintenance. The light can be triggered due to a variety of issues, such as low oil pressure, low coolant levels, or a malfunctioning oxygen sensor.

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light</h2>

Resetting the maintenance light on the 2007 Toyota Camry XLE is simple and can be done in a few easy steps.

<h3>Step 1: Open the Hood</h3>
The first step is to open the hood of the car and locate the fuse box. The fuse box is typically located on the driver's side of the engine bay.

<h3>Step 2: Locate the Fuse</h3>
Once you have located the fuse box, you will need to locate the correct fuse. The correct fuse for resetting the maintenance light is labeled "MAINT." Once you have located the correct fuse, remove it from the fuse box.

<h3>Step 3: Wait for 60 Seconds</h3>
The next step is to wait for 60 seconds before reinserting the fuse. This will allow the car's computer to reset and the light to go off.

<h3>Step 4: Reinsert the Fuse</h3>
After waiting 60 seconds, reinsert the fuse into the fuse box and close the hood. The maintenance light should now be reset.
