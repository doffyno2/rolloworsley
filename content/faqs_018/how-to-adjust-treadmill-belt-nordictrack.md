<p>When you own a NordicTrack treadmill, you want to make sure it is in its best condition. Taking care of it includes regularly cleaning and servicing it. One of the most important things you should do is adjusting the belt of your treadmill. Adjusting the belt ensures that the treadmill is running smoothly and efficiently. </p>

<p>When adjusting a treadmill belt, you need to do it in a certain way. If it is not done correctly, it can cause damage to the belt or motor. Here are the steps you need to take to adjust the belt on a NordicTrack treadmill: </p>

<h2>Step 1: Unplug the Treadmill</h2> 

<p>The first thing you need to do is unplug the treadmill. This is important to make sure that you are not electrocuted while adjusting the belt. </p>

<h2>Step 2: Locate the Adjustment Screws</h2> 

<p>Next, you need to locate the adjustment screws. On the NordicTrack treadmill, the adjustment screws are located near the front and rear rollers. </p> 

<h2>Step 3: Loosen the Rear Roller</h2> 

<p>Once you have located the adjustment screws, you need to loosen the rear roller. This is done by turning the adjustment screw counterclockwise. </p>

<h2>Step 4: Tighten the Front Roller</h2> 

<p>Once you have loosened the rear roller, you need to tighten the front roller. This is done by turning the adjustment screw clockwise. </p>

<h2>Step 5: Check the Tension</h2> 

<p>Once you have tightened the front roller, you need to check the tension of the belt. You can do this by pushing down on the belt with your hand. The belt should have some resistance, but not too much. If the belt is too tight, you will need to loosen the rear roller a bit more. </p>

<h2>Step 6: Check the Alignment</h2> 

<p>Finally, you need to check the alignment of the belt. You can do this by looking at the belt while the treadmill is running. The belt should be centered between the two rollers. If it is not, you will need to adjust the front and rear rollers until the belt is centered. </p> 

<p>These are the steps you need to take to adjust the belt on a NordicTrack treadmill. It is important to do this correctly in order to keep your treadmill running smoothly and efficiently. </p>
