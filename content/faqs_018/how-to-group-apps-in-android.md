<p>Android is the most popular mobile operating system in the world, and it offers a wide range of features and customization options. One of the most useful features of Android is the ability to group apps together in folders, which makes it easier to organize and manage your apps. In this article, we'll show you how to group apps in Android so you can quickly and easily access your favorite apps.</p>

<h2>What Is App Grouping?</h2>

<p>App grouping is a feature of Android that allows you to organize your apps into folders. This makes it easier to access your favorite apps, as you don't have to scroll through all of your apps to find the one you need. This also helps keep your home screen organized and clutter-free.</p>

<h2>How to Group Apps in Android</h2>

<p>Grouping apps in Android is a simple and straightforward process. All you need to do is follow these steps:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Open the App Drawer</h3>

<p>The first step is to open the app drawer, which is the list of all of your apps. If you're using a stock Android device, the app drawer will be located on the home screen. If you're using a third-party launcher, it may be located in a different place.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Select the Apps You Want to Group</h3>

<p>Once you've opened the app drawer, you can select the apps that you want to group together. To do this, simply long-press on the app icon and then select the apps that you want to group together.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Create the Folder</h3>

<p>Once you've selected the apps that you want to group together, you can create the folder by dragging one of the apps onto the other. This will create a folder with all of the apps that you selected. You can then give the folder a name and add an icon.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) About App Grouping</h2>

<h3>Q: How do I add apps to an existing folder?</h3>

<p>A: To add apps to an existing folder, simply open the folder and then long-press on the app icon that you want to add. This will add the app to the folder.</p>

<h3>Q: How do I delete a folder?</h3>

<p>A: To delete a folder, simply open the folder and then tap the "Delete" button. This will delete the folder and all of its contents.</p>

<h3>Q: Can I create folders within folders?</h3>

<p>A: Yes, you can create folders within folders in Android. To do this, simply create a folder and then drag the folder that you want to create inside of it. This will create a nested folder.</p>

<h3>Q: Are folders visible on the home screen?</h3>

<p>A: Yes, folders are visible on the home screen of Android devices. However, you can choose to hide them if you don't want them to be visible.</p>

<h3>Q: Can I move folders between screens?</h3>

<p>A: Yes, you can move folders between screens in Android. To do this, simply long-press on the folder and then drag it to the desired screen.</p>

<h3>Q: Can I change the icon of a folder?</h3>

<p>A: Yes, you can change the icon of a folder. To do this, open the folder and then tap the "Change Icon" button. This will allow you to select a new icon for the folder.</p>
