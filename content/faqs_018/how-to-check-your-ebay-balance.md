<h2>What is an eBay Balance?</h2>

An eBay balance is the amount of money that an eBay user has in their account. It is the money that is available for buying items on the eBay marketplace. This balance is updated when you make a purchase or when you receive money from a sale on eBay.

<h2>How to Check Your eBay Balance?</h2>

Checking your eBay balance is easy and can be done quickly using the eBay website or the eBay app.

<h3>Using the eBay Website</h3>

1. Log in to the eBay website with your username and password.
2. Click on the “My eBay” tab at the top of the page.
3. Click on “Account” in the top right corner of the page.
4. You will then see a summary of your eBay account, including your current balance.

<h3>Using the eBay App</h3>

1. Open the eBay app on your smartphone or tablet.
2. Log in to the app using your username and password.
3. Tap the “My eBay” tab at the bottom of the page.
4. You will then see a summary of your eBay account, including your current balance.

<h3>How to Add Money to Your eBay Balance?</h3>

You can add money to your eBay balance by making a payment through PayPal or by using a debit or credit card. You can also add money to your balance by transferring funds from your bank account.
