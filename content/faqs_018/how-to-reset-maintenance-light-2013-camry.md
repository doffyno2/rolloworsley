<h3>Step 1: Locate the Reset Button </h3>

<h3>Step 2: Press and Hold the Reset Button </h3>

<p>Once you’ve located the reset button, press and hold it for at least 5 seconds. You should hear a beep when the light is reset. If you don’t hear a beep, try pressing and holding the button again. </p>

<h3>Step 3: Turn Off the Ignition </h3>

<p>Once the maintenance light has been reset, turn off the ignition and wait for the light to go off. If it doesn’t go off, try repeating steps 1 and 2. </p>
