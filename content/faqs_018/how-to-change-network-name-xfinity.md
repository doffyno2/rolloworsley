Changing the network name of your Xfinity router can be done in a few simple steps.</p>

<h2>Steps for Changing the Network Name of Your Xfinity Router</h2>

<p>When you are ready to change the network name of your Xfinity router, here are the steps you need to take:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Open the Xfinity Router Settings Page</h3>

<p>The first step to changing the network name of your Xfinity router is to open the router settings page. To do this, open your web browser on a computer or laptop that is connected to your Xfinity router. Then, type in the IP address of your Xfinity router. The IP address is usually listed on the bottom of your Xfinity router.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Enter Your Login Details</h3>

<p>Once you have entered the IP address of your Xfinity router, you will be prompted to enter your login details. The username and password are usually printed on the bottom of your Xfinity router. If you have changed the default username and password, you will need to enter the new details.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Find the Network Name Settings</h3>

<p>Once you have logged into the Xfinity router settings page, you will need to find the network name settings. This is usually located in the "Wireless" or "Network" tab. Click on the tab and then look for the section that says "Network Name" or "SSID".</p>

<h3>Step 4: Enter the New Network Name</h3>

<p>Once you have found the network name settings, you can enter your new network name. Make sure to choose a network name that is unique and easy to remember. Once you have entered the new network name, click "Apply" to save the changes.</p>

<h3>Step 5: Test the New Network Name</h3>

<p>Once you have saved the changes, you can test the new network name. To do this, turn off your computer or laptop and then turn it back on. Then, open a web browser and search for the new network name. If you see the new name listed in the available networks, then you have successfully changed the network name.</p>
