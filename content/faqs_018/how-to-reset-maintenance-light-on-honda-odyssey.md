Once you have located the reset button, the next step is to push and hold the reset button for at least three seconds. This will reset the maintenance light and turn it off. 

<h2>Step 3: Verify the Light is Off</h2>

The final step is to verify that the maintenance light is now off. To do this, start the engine and check the instrument panel. If the light is off, then the reset process was successful and you can now proceed with normal vehicle operation.
