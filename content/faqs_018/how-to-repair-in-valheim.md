<h2>What is Repairing in Valheim?</h2>

Repairing in Valheim is the process of restoring the durability of an item. All items in the game have a set amount of durability and can be crafted or purchased. When an item’s durability drops to zero, it will break and become unusable. Repairing restores the item’s durability, allowing it to be used again.

<h2>How to Repair in Valheim</h2>

Repairing an item in Valheim is a simple process. All players need is a workbench and the required resources. To repair an item, players must open the workbench and select the “Repair” tab. From here, they must select the item they wish to repair and the resources required to do so. Once the resources have been selected, the item will be repaired and ready to use.

<h2>What Resources are Needed to Repair Items in Valheim?</h2>

The resources needed to repair an item in Valheim vary depending on the type of item. For example, armor and weapons require Iron, while tools and furniture require Wood. The amount of resources needed also varies depending on the item’s current durability. The more durability an item has lost, the more resources will be required to repair it.
