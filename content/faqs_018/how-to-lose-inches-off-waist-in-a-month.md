<h2>How Body Fat Affects Your Waistline</h2>

<p>When you are trying to lose inches off your waist, it’s important to understand how body fat works. Body fat is made up of two types of fat, subcutaneous and visceral. Subcutaneous fat is the layer of fat that is just beneath the skin, while visceral fat is stored deeper in the body. Visceral fat is the type of fat that is most dangerous because it is stored around the internal organs, such as the liver, pancreas, and intestines. Excess visceral fat can lead to an increased risk of heart disease, stroke, and type 2 diabetes.</p>
