<h3>Step 2: Locate the Maintenance Light</h3>

Once the engine is running, you can locate the maintenance light. It will be located on the dashboard near the speedometer.

<h3>Step 3: Press and Hold the Reset Button</h3>

Once the maintenance light is located, press and hold the reset button for four seconds. This will reset the maintenance light and turn it off.

<h3>Step 4: Turn Off the Ignition</h3>
