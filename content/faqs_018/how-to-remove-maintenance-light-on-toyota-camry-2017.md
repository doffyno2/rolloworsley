<h2>What is the Maintenance Light?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light is an indicator light that appears on your dashboard when it's time for your vehicle to have a regular maintenance check. It's typically a yellow or orange light that's shaped like a wrench or screwdriver. When it comes on, it's time for your vehicle to have a full service. It's important to reset the maintenance light after you've completed the service, so you can be sure that your vehicle is running as safely and efficiently as possible.</p>

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on the Toyota Camry 2017</h2>

<p>Resetting the maintenance light on the Toyota Camry 2017 is a fairly straightforward process. First, make sure that the vehicle is in park, and that the engine is off. Then, locate the reset button on the dashboard. It should be located near the steering wheel, and is typically labeled "Maintenance Reset" or "Service Reset". Press and hold the button for a few seconds until the light turns off. That's it - your maintenance light is now reset and you're good to go!</p>
