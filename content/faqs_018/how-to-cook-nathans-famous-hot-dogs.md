<p>Nathan's Famous Hot Dogs are an iconic American food, loved by many for their unique, juicy flavor. Since 1916, Nathan's has been serving up hot dogs that are unlike any other. <h2>Ingredients</h2>

<p>The most important part of making Nathan's famous hot dogs is getting the right ingredients. To make Nathan's style hot dogs, you will need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Hot dogs</li>
  <li>Buns</li>
  <li>Mustard</li>
  <li>Ketchup</li>
  <li>Onions</li>
  <li>Relish</li>
</ul>

<h2>Cooking Techniques</h2>

<p>Once you have the right ingredients, it's time to start cooking. Nathan's hot dogs are best cooked on a griddle or in a skillet. To get the best results, make sure to preheat the griddle or skillet before adding the hot dogs. Cook the hot dogs for about 10 minutes, flipping them occasionally for even cooking. When the hot dogs are done, the skin should be golden brown and the inside should be hot and juicy.</p>

<h2>Condiments</h2>

<p>Once the hot dogs are cooked, it's time to add the condiments. Nathan's hot dogs are usually served with mustard, ketchup, onions and relish. Start by spreading a generous amount of mustard and ketchup on the bun. Then, top the hot dogs with the onions and relish. The onions should be sliced very thin, and the relish should be sweet. If desired, you can also add other condiments, such as cheese and chili.</p>

<h2>Serving</h2>

<p>Once the hot dogs are topped with condiments, it's time to serve them. Nathan's hot dogs are usually served on a plate or in a basket, along with a side of fries or potato chips. If desired, you can also serve them with a side of coleslaw or onion rings. Hot dogs are best served immediately after cooking, so make sure to serve them while they are still hot.</p>
