<h2>What You'll Need</h2>

<p>To fold a Privacy Pop bed tent, you'll need the tent itself, a pair of scissors, and a ruler. You'll also need a flat surface that is large enough to accommodate the tent when unfolded. Finally, you'll need a Phillips head screwdriver.</p>

<h2>Step 1: Unfold the Tent</h2>

<p>First, you'll need to unfold the tent so that it lays flat on your work surface. Make sure to remove any accessories that have been attached to the tent, such as wires or duct tape. Once the tent is unfolded, use the scissors to cut away any excess material. </p>

<h2>Step 2: Measure and Cut</h2>

<p>Using the ruler, measure the length and width of the tent. Then, use the scissors to cut away any excess material. Cut along the seams, and make sure to cut away any excess fabric or thread. Once you've finished cutting away the excess material, you should be left with a rectangle-shaped tent.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Fold the Tent</h2>

<p>The next step is to fold the tent. Start by folding the long sides of the tent inwards. Then, fold the short sides of the tent inwards. Make sure to keep the folds neat and even. Once you have finished folding the tent, it should be in the shape of a small rectangle.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Secure the Tent</h2>

<p>Now, it's time to secure the tent. Use a Phillips head screwdriver to tighten the screws that were included with the tent. These screws will help keep the tent securely in place. Once all of the screws are tightened, your Privacy Pop bed tent is ready to be used!</p>
