<p>Finding your group number for health insurance is an important step in understanding your plan. <p>If you have health coverage through your employer, your group number is typically provided by your employer. <p>If you purchased your own health insurance policy, your group number will be listed on your policy paperwork. Sometimes it may be listed in the section titled “Group Number” or “Policy Number”.
