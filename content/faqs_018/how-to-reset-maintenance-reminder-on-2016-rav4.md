<h2>What is the Maintenance Reminder?</h2>

The maintenance reminder is a system that is built into your 2016 Rav4. It is designed to alert you when it is time for you to take your car in for a checkup or to get oil changes. The maintenance reminder is triggered by sensors that are located in the engine, and it can be reset manually or automatically. 

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Reminder Manually</h2>

The first step in resetting the maintenance reminder is to locate the reset button. The reset button is located on the dashboard near the steering wheel. Once you have located the reset button, press it firmly and hold it down until the maintenance reminder light turns off. Once the light is off, the maintenance reminder has been reset and it is ready to be used again.

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Reminder Automatically</h2>

The second way to reset the maintenance reminder is to use the Automatic Maintenance Reset feature. This feature is available on some 2016 Rav4 models and it allows you to reset the maintenance reminder without having to press the reset button. To use the Automatic Maintenance Reset feature, simply turn off the engine and wait for the maintenance reminder light to turn off. When the light turns off, the maintenance reminder has been reset and it is ready to be used again.
