<h2>What is the Maintenance Light?</h2>

The Maintenance Light on the 2010 Toyota Corolla is a warning light that illuminates when the vehicle needs to be serviced. It is usually triggered by a certain number of miles or by a certain number of months since the last service. The Maintenance Light helps to remind the driver to take the vehicle in for regular maintenance as recommended by the manufacturer.

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2010 Toyota Corolla</h2>

In order to reset the Maintenance Light on a 2010 Toyota Corolla, there are a few simple steps that must be followed. First, turn the ignition switch to the OFF position. Then, press and hold the trip reset button for about 10 seconds. Finally, release the trip reset button and the Maintenance Light will be reset. It is important to note that the Maintenance Light will remain illuminated until the vehicle is serviced at the recommended intervals.

After resetting the Maintenance Light on a 2010 Toyota Corolla, it is important to take the vehicle in for regular maintenance.
