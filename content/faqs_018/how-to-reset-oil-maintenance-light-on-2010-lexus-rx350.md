<h2>Understanding the Oil Maintenance Light</h2>

The oil maintenance light on a Lexus RX350 is an indicator light that informs you when it is time to change your oil. This light is usually located on the dashboard and will be illuminated when it is time to change the oil. It is important to reset the oil maintenance light after each oil change to ensure the light will turn on again after the next oil change interval. Knowing how to reset the oil maintenance light on a 2010 Lexus RX350 can save you time and money.

<h2>Instructions for Resetting the Oil Maintenance Light</h2>

1. Start the engine and let it idle for a few minutes to allow the oil to circulate and warm up.

2. Place the gear selector in park and turn the engine off.

3. Open the hood and locate the oil dipstick.

4. Pull the dipstick out of the engine and wipe it clean with a cloth or paper towel.

5. Reinsert the dipstick and make sure it is fully seated.

6. Turn the ignition switch to the “ON” position, but do not start the engine.

7. Press and hold the “TRIP” button on the instrument panel until the maintenance light starts blinking.

8. Release the “TRIP” button and the maintenance light will turn off.

9. Start the engine and the oil maintenance light reset process is complete.
