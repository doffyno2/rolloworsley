<h2>What Are Calling Cards?</h2>

<p>Calling cards are items you can send to other characters in Persona 5. They serve as a way to communicate with other characters in the game, and can be used to start conversations, or to ask for favors. You can also send calling cards to other characters to request items or money. Each calling card has a unique design, which reflects the character it is sent from.</p>

<p>In Persona 5, you can find calling cards at the Vending Machine in Shibuya. You can also purchase them from the Velvet Room. <h2>How to Send Calling Cards</h2>

<p>Once you have a calling card, you can send it to other characters in Persona 5. To do this, open the pause menu, and select the “Send Calling Card” option. You will then be prompted to select a character to send the card to. Once you’ve selected a character, you will be asked to confirm the selection. After you’ve done that, the calling card will be sent.</p>
