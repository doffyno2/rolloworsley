<h2>What is a Sway Bar Link End?</h2>

<p>A sway bar link end is a component of a car's suspension system. It is connected to the sway bar, which is a long metal bar that is mounted between the left and right wheels. The sway bar helps to reduce body roll when cornering by transferring the force from one side of the car to the other. The sway bar link end is connected to the sway bar and helps to keep it in place.</p>

<h2>What Causes Damage to Sway Bar Link Ends?</h2>

<p>Sway bar link ends can be damaged by regular wear and tear, or by a collision or other impact. They can also be damaged by corrosion, or by debris such as rocks or other road objects that can get lodged in the link end. In some cases, the link end may simply be worn out due to age.</p>
