<h2>What is a Presta Valve Core?</h2>

<p>A Presta valve core is a small, cylindrical piece of metal that is inserted into the Presta valve stem. Its purpose is to regulate the flow of air in and out of the tube. It is important to periodically check the condition of the valve core and replace it if necessary.</p>

<h3>Step 1: Unscrew the Top of the Valve</h3>
