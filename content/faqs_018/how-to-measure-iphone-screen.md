<p>The iPhone is one of the most popular smartphones in the world, and it's important to keep the screen in working order. One of the best ways to do this is to measure the screen on your iPhone to make sure it is the correct size. Knowing the exact size of your iPhone's screen can help you find the right cases, accessories, and more.</p>

<h2>Why Measure Your iPhone Screen?</h2>

<p>Measuring your iPhone's screen is important for a number of reasons. It can help you find the right case and accessories, including screen protectors and cases that are the right size for your device. It can also help you find the right screen protector, since many are designed to fit a specific size. Knowing the exact dimensions of your iPhone's screen can also help you find a good, snug fit when you're attaching a case or screen protector.</p>

<h2>How to Measure iPhone Screen?</h2>

<p>Measuring your iPhone's screen is relatively simple. Here are the steps:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Turn off your iPhone</h3>

<p>Turn off your iPhone by pressing and holding the power button until the “slide to power off” appears on the screen. Slide the slider to the right to turn off your device.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Measure the width of the screen</h3>

<p>Using a ruler or measuring tape, measure the width of the iPhone's screen from left to right. Make sure to measure from the bezel to the bezel, rather than from the edge of the phone to the opposite edge of the phone.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Measure the height of the screen</h3>

<p>Next, measure the height of the iPhone's screen from top to bottom. Again, make sure to measure from the bezel to the bezel, rather than from the edge of the phone to the opposite edge of the phone.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Record your measurements</h3>

<p>Once you have measured the width and height of the iPhone's screen, record the measurements in a safe place. This will help you find the right case, screen protector, and other accessories that are the right size for your device.</p>
