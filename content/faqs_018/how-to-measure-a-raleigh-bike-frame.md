<p>Are you looking to buy a Raleigh bike frame? Measurement is a crucial component of purchasing a bike frame, and it's important to ensure you get the right size for your body. This guide will walk you through the process of measuring a Raleigh bike frame.</p>

<h2>What You'll Need</h2>

<p>Before you can measure a Raleigh bike frame, you'll need to gather the following items:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Tape measure</li>
  <li>Pen and paper</li>
  <li>Raleigh bike frame</li>
</ul>

<h2>Step 1: Measure the Top Tube Length</h2>

<p>The top tube length is the distance between the head tube and the seat tube. To measure it, start by finding the midpoint of the top tube (where it meets the seat tube). Use the tape measure to measure the distance between the midpoint and the head tube.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Measure the Seat Tube Length</h2>

<p>The seat tube length is the distance between the bottom bracket and the seat tube. To measure it, start by finding the midpoint of the seat tube (where it meets the bottom bracket). Use the tape measure to measure the distance between the midpoint and the top tube.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Measure the Head Tube Height</h2>

<p>The head tube height is the distance between the head tube and the ground. To measure it, start by finding the midpoint of the head tube. Use the tape measure to measure the distance between the midpoint and the ground.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Calculate the Standover Height</h2>

<p>The standover height is the distance between the top tube and the ground. To calculate it, start by measuring the distance between the bottom bracket and the top tube. Then subtract the distance between the head tube and the bottom bracket. The result is the standover height.</p>

<h2>Step 5: Measure the Chainstay Length</h2>

<p>The chainstay length is the distance between the bottom bracket and the rear axle. To measure it, start by finding the midpoint of the chainstay (where it meets the bottom bracket). Use the tape measure to measure the distance between the midpoint and the rear axle.</p>

<h2>Step 6: Measure the Bottom Bracket Drop</h2>

<p>The bottom bracket drop is the distance between the bottom bracket and the ground. To measure it, start by finding the midpoint of the bottom bracket. Use the tape measure to measure the distance between the midpoint and the ground.</p>
