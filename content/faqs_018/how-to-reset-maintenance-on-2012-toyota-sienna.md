<h2>Step 1: Locate the Maintenance Light</h2>

<p>The first step to resetting the maintenance light on a 2012 Toyota Sienna is to locate the maintenance light itself. The maintenance light is typically located on the dashboard, near the speedometer, and is usually a small yellow or orange light with a wrench icon. If the light is not already illuminated, press and hold the “Set” button on the dashboard to turn it on.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Turn the Ignition Key</h2>

<p>Once the light is illuminated, turn the ignition key to the “On” position. This will activate the maintenance light reset procedure. Do not start the engine.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Press and Hold the Reset Button</h2>

<p>With the ignition key in the “On” position, press and hold the “Set” button on the dashboard for at least five seconds. This will reset the maintenance light and turn it off. The maintenance light should remain off until the next maintenance interval. If the light does not turn off after five seconds, repeat the process.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Turn the Ignition Key Off</h2>

<p>Once the maintenance light has been reset, turn the ignition key off and remove it from the vehicle. The maintenance light should remain off until the next maintenance interval.</p>
