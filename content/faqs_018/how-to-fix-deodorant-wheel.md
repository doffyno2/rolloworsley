<p>When a deodorant wheel becomes stuck, it is usually due to a buildup of dirt and grime. Over time, the wheel can become clogged with dirt and grime, which can prevent it from turning. Other causes of deodorant wheel issues include lack of lubrication and improper use.</p>

<h2>How to Fix a Stuck Deodorant Wheel</h2>

<p>The first step in fixing a stuck deodorant wheel is to remove any dirt or grime that has built up. To do this, use a clean cloth or brush to carefully remove any debris that is stuck to the wheel.
