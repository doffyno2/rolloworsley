How to Cook Taquitos on Hot Dog Roller


Taquitos are a popular Mexican dish made of rolled tortillas filled with cheese, meat, and spices. They are easy to make and are great for parties or as a light snack. Traditionally, taquitos are fried in oil on a stovetop, but if you don't want to deal with the mess and hassle of frying, you can also cook them on a hot dog roller. Hot dog rollers are designed to evenly cook hot dogs, but they can also be used to cook taquitos. In this article, we will discuss how to cook taquitos on a hot dog roller.



To cook taquitos on a hot dog roller, you will need the following items:

<ul>
  <li>Hot dog roller</li>
  <li>Tortillas</li>
  <li>Filling of your choice (cheese, meat, etc.)</li>
  <li>Oil (for brushing the tortillas)</li>
  <li>Spatula</li>
  <li>Paper towels</li>
</ul>



<h2>Step 1: Prepare the Filling</h2>

The first step is to prepare the filling for your taquitos. You can use any type of filling you like, such as cheese, meat, vegetables, etc. Make sure the filling is well-seasoned and flavorful.

<h2>Step 2: Prepare the Tortillas</h2>

Once the filling is ready, you can start preparing the tortillas. Take a tortilla and spread a thin layer of oil on one side. This will help the tortillas to become crispy when cooked.

<h2>Step 3: Assemble the Taquitos</h2>

Now it's time to assemble the taquitos. Take a tortilla and spread a layer of the filling on one side. Roll the tortilla up tightly and place it on the hot dog roller. Repeat this process until all of the taquitos are assembled.

<h2>Step 4: Cook the Taquitos</h2>

Once all of the taquitos are assembled, you can start cooking. Turn the hot dog roller on and let it preheat for a few minutes. When the roller is hot, carefully place the taquitos onto the hot dog roller. Cook the taquitos for about 10 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

<h2>Step 5: Serve the Taquitos</h2>

Once the taquitos are cooked, carefully remove them from the hot dog roller with a spatula. Place them on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess oil. Serve the taquitos immediately with your favorite toppings and sauces. Enjoy!
