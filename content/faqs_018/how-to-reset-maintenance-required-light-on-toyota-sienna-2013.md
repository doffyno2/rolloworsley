<h2>What is the Maintenance Required Light?</h2>

<p>The maintenance required light is a dashboard indicator light that appears in Toyota Sienna 2013 models. It is a reminder to get your vehicle serviced and checked for any maintenance needed. This light will illuminate when it is time for your vehicle to be serviced. It is important that you reset the maintenance required light after servicing your vehicle or performing any maintenance.</p>

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Required Light on a Toyota Sienna 2013</h2>

<p>Resetting the maintenance required light on a Toyota Sienna 2013 is a simple task. The following steps will guide you through the process.</p>

<h3>Step 1: Turn on the ignition.</h3>

<p>Turn the ignition on without starting the engine. The maintenance required light should be illuminated on the dashboard.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Press and hold the trip reset button.</h3>

<p>Press and hold the trip reset button for about 10 seconds. This button is located on the instrument panel, just below the speedometer.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Release the trip reset button.</h3>

<p>Release the trip reset button once you have held it for 10 seconds. The maintenance required light should be off.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Turn off the ignition.</h3>

<p>Turn the ignition off. You have now successfully reset the maintenance required light on your Toyota Sienna 2013.</p>
