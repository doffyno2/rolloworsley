<p>Myungrang hot dog is a popular South Korean street food consisting of a grilled sausage in a hot dog bun topped with sweet and spicy chili sauce, mustard, mayonnaise, and kimchi. It's a delicious snack that can be enjoyed any time of day. In this article, we'll explain how to make this delicious dish at home.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed</h2>

<p>To make Myungrang hot dog, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 package of hot dog buns</li>
  <li>1 package of hot dogs</li>
  <li>1 bottle of sweet and spicy chili sauce</li>
  <li>1 bottle of mustard</li>
  <li>1 bottle of mayonnaise</li>
  <li>1 package of kimchi</li>
</ul>

<h2>Instructions</h2>

<p>Making Myungrang hot dog is easy. First, preheat your oven to 350°F. Then, open the package of hot dog buns and place them on a baking sheet. Place the hot dogs on top of the buns and bake for 15 minutes. Once the hot dogs are cooked, remove them from the oven.</p>

<p>Next, prepare the sauce by combining the chili sauce, mustard, and mayonnaise in a small bowl. Stir until the ingredients are well-mixed. Then, spread the sauce evenly over the hot dogs.</p>

<p>Finally, top the hot dogs with kimchi. You can also add other toppings such as diced onions, pickles, or jalapenos if desired. Serve the Myungrang hot dog immediately.</p>
