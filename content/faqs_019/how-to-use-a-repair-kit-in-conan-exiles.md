<h2>What is a Repair Kit?</h2>

<p>A repair kit is a consumable item in Conan Exiles which allows players to repair broken items. It can be crafted using a variety of materials, such as leather, cloth, and metal. The repair kit will restore the item’s durability, allowing it to be used again. The repair kit can be used on weapons, armor, and other equipment. It can also be used to repair buildings and other structures.</p>

<h2>How to Craft a Repair Kit</h2>

<p>Repair kits can be crafted at the Smithy. To craft a repair kit, you will need the following ingredients: 
<ul>
<li>2 Leather Strips</li>
<li>2 Cloth Strips</li>
<li>2 Metal Bars</li>
</ul>

Once you have the ingredients, you can craft the repair kit using the Smithy. You can find the Smithy in the Crafting tab of the Inventory screen.</p>

<h2>How to Use a Repair Kit</h2>

<p>To use a repair kit, equip it in your inventory and select it. You will then be able to select an item to repair. The item will be restored to full durability and can be used again. However, keep in mind that a repair kit can only be used once, so make sure to use it wisely. It is also important to note that repair kits cannot be used on buildings and structures.</p>

<h2>Where to Find Repair Kits</h2>

<p>Repair kits can be found as random loot in the world, as rewards for completing quests, and can also be purchased from merchants. They can also be crafted at the Smithy. If you’re having trouble finding repair kits, you can check the Marketplace tab in your inventory screen.</p>
