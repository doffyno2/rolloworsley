W stands for waist size. This measurement is taken by measuring the circumference of the waist of the pants. The measurement is taken at the narrowest part of the waist, usually in the front. <h2>What Does L Stand For?</h2>

L stands for length. This measurement is taken by measuring the length of the legs of the jeans. It is important to note that the length measurement is taken from the top of the waistband to the bottom of the hem.
