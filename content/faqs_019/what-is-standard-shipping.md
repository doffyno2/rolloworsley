<h2>How Do I Track My Standard Shipping Order?</h2>

Most online retailers provide tracking numbers with standard shipping orders. You can use the tracking number to track your order online or by phone. If your order was shipped with a carrier other than USPS, you can also track it on their website or by calling the carrier directly.

<h2>How Can I Save Money on Standard Shipping?</h2>
