The equator is an imaginary line that runs around the middle of the Earth, halfway between the North Pole and the South Pole. It's the line of latitude that divides the world into the Northern Hemisphere and the Southern Hemisphere. The line runs through the Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Oceans.
