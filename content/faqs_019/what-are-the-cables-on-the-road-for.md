<h2>What Are the Types of Cables on the Road?</h2>

The cables on the road are typically either low voltage or high voltage. Low voltage cables are typically used for street lighting and traffic signals, while high voltage cables are used for power transmission and telephone lines. The type of cable used depends on the type of application, and the amount of power needed.

<h2>Where Are Cables Installed?</h2>

Cables are usually installed along the side of the road in underground conduits. The conduits provide a safe and secure way to protect the cables from damage and potential theft. Many cities also have overhead cables which are installed on poles along the side of the road.
