<h2>Look For Visible Signs of Spoilage</h2>

<p>The most obvious sign that hot dogs have gone bad is visible spoilage. If you see any mold growing on the hot dogs, they should be discarded immediately. <p>It's also important to check the packaging of the hot dogs for any changes. If the packaging is swollen or the seal is broken, the hot dogs should be discarded. This is an indication that the hot dogs have gone bad and may contain harmful bacteria.</p>

<p>Another way to tell if hot dogs have gone bad is to smell them. If the hot dogs smell off or sour, they should not be eaten. If the smell is strong and unpleasant, the hot dogs should be discarded.</p>
