What Is a Backpack Lash Tab?

A backpack lash tab is a small piece of fabric or webbing located on the outside of a backpack. This tab is used for attaching additional items to the outside of the bag, freeing up space inside the bag for other essential gear and supplies. The tab is usually located on the bottom of the bag, just above the waist belt. It can also be found on the side or top of a backpack, depending on the design.

How to Use a Backpack Lash Tab

Using a backpack lash tab is simple. All you need is a carabiner or some other type of clip to attach the gear you want to carry to the tab. Most carabiners have a loop at one end that you can easily slip over the tab.
