<h2>What is Prednisone?</h2>

Prednisone is a steroid medication that is used to treat a wide range of health issues. It works by suppressing the body’s immune system, reducing inflammation and pain. It is commonly prescribed to treat allergies, asthma, skin conditions, arthritis, and some types of cancer.

<h2>Can Humans Take Prednisone for Dogs?</h2>

The short answer is yes – humans can take prednisone for dogs, but only under the supervision of a doctor. Prednisone is a powerful medication, and while it is generally safe for dogs, it can have some serious side effects in humans.
