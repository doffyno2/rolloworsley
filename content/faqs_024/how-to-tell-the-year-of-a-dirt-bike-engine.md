<h2>Examine the Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)</h2>

<p>The Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) is etched into the frame of the dirt bike and can be used to tell the year of the engine. The VIN is a 17-digit number that can be used to determine the engine's make, model, and year. You can find the VIN on the frame near the head tube, or on the left side of the engine. It may also be found on the title or registration.</p>

<h2>Find the Engine Number</h2>

<p>You can also use the engine number to determine the year of the dirt bike engine. The engine number is usually located on the left side of the engine, near the carburetor. If you look closely, you should be able to find a number that is stamped into the metal. This number is unique to the motorcycle, and can be used to determine the year of manufacture.</p>
