<h2>Step 1: Access the Settings</h2>

<p>The first step to turning off your Versa watch is to access the settings. You can do this by pressing and holding the back button on the right side of the watch. This will open a menu where you can select the “Settings” option.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Find the Power Off Option</h2>

<p>Once you have accessed the settings, you will need to find the power off option. This can be found in the “Device” menu. You can access this menu by scrolling down the list of settings. Once you have found the “Device” menu, you should be able to find the power off option.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Select the Power Off Option</h2>

<p>Once you have accessed the power off option, you can select it. This will open a window that will ask you to confirm that you want to turn off your Versa watch. Select yes to confirm.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Turn Off Your Versa Watch</h2>

<p>Once you have selected the power off option, your Versa watch will turn off. You will need to wait a few moments for the watch to power down completely before you can turn it back on again. When the watch is completely powered down, it will no longer bother you with notifications.</p>
