<h2>What Is a Trucker Hat?</h2>

A trucker hat is a type of hat that is typically constructed of foam or mesh fabric and has a wide, curved brim. The hats are often decorated with a patch or logo on the front, and most often have a snapback closure for adjustability. Trucker hats have been popular since the 1970s and are now a fashion staple.

<h2>Why Stretch a Trucker Hat?</h2>
