<h2>What is a Diaphragm Turkey Call?</h2>

A diaphragm turkey call is a type of turkey call used to lure in wild turkeys. It is made from a thin, flexible material such as latex or rubber, and is held in the mouth. The hunter blows into the call, creating a variety of sounds that imitate a turkey's mating calls. Diaphragm turkey calls are popular among hunters because they are easy to use and make a variety of realistic turkey sounds.

In addition, diaphragm turkey calls are relatively inexpensive and can be used in any weather conditions.
