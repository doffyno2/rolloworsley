<p>A parts washer is a machine used to clean parts and components. The parts washer is designed to clean small parts and components that cannot be cleaned by hand. <h2>How Does a Parts Washer Work?</h2>

<p>Parts washers use a combination of water and a cleaning solution to clean parts and components. The parts washer has a tank that holds the water and cleaning solution.
