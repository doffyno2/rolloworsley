Trailforks is an online trail database that provides its users with detailed information about trails all around the world. It’s a great resource for anyone looking to find trails, create routes, or get the latest trail conditions. In this article, we’ll discuss how to use Trailforks and the various features available.

<h2>Creating an Account</h2>

The first step to using Trailforks is creating an account. This will allow you to access the full features of the website, and make sure that your data is backed up and accessible. To create an account, first visit the Trailforks website and click “Sign Up” in the top right corner. You’ll be asked to provide your email address and a password of your choice, and then you’ll be ready to get started.

<h2>Finding Trails</h2>

Once you have your account created, you can start exploring the trails available on Trailforks. To find a trail near you, simply enter your city, state, or zip code in the search bar at the top of the page. You’ll then be presented with a list of trails in your area, sorted by distance. You can also use the map view to see trails in your area, and you can filter them by type (hiking, mountain biking, etc.)

<h2>Viewing Trail Details</h2>

When you click on a trail in the list or on the map, you’ll be presented with the detailed information about that trail. This includes the trail description, photos, videos, and a list of nearby trails. You can also view the trail’s difficulty rating, elevation profile, and user-submitted comments.

<h2>Creating a Route</h2>

If you’re looking to plan a route, Trailforks makes it easy. You can search for trails in the same way as before, and then click the “Create Route” button. From there, you can add trails to your route, and use the map view to see the elevation profile and total distance. You can also save your route for future reference.

<h2>Using the Mobile App</h2>

Trailforks also has a mobile app for both iOS and Android devices. The app allows you to view and search for trails, and use the same features as the website. You can also track your rides with the app, and view real-time stats such as speed, distance, and elevation gain.

<h2>Sharing Your Rides</h2>

You can also share your rides with the Trailforks community. Simply create a ride, and then you can upload photos, videos, and comments to let other users know how your ride went. You can also join other rides or challenges, and compare your rides with other users.
