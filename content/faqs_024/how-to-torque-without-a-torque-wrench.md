<h2>What is Torque?</h2>

<p>Torque is a measure of the rotational force applied to a fastener, such as a bolt or a nut. It is measured in units of foot-pounds or Newton-meters. The amount of torque required to properly tighten a fastener depends on the size of the fastener, the material it is made of, and the surface it is being tightened against.</p>

<h2>What is a Torque Wrench?</h2>

<p>A torque wrench is a tool used to apply a specific amount of torque to a fastener. It consists of a handle attached to a calibrated shaft that is marked with torque values. The user applies pressure to the handle, which is transferred to the shaft, causing it to rotate. The amount of torque applied is determined by the amount of pressure applied to the handle.</p>
