<h2>Why is Green the Mental Health Awareness Color?</h2>

Green is the global color for mental health awareness because of its association with nature, growth, and hope. Green is the color of life, renewal, and energy. It is also the color of balance, which is something that those dealing with mental health issues strive for. The color green is thought to be calming and can bring about a sense of peace and tranquility, which can be helpful for those dealing with mental health issues.
