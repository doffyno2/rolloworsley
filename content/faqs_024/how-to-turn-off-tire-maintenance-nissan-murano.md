<h2>What Does the Tire Maintenance Light Mean?</h2>

<p>The tire maintenance light is a warning indicator that appears when the tire pressure of your Nissan Murano needs to be adjusted. This is usually due to worn out tires, which can be dangerous and cause a loss of traction. When the tire maintenance light comes on, it’s important to take action to address the issue. </p>

<h2>How to Turn Off the Tire Maintenance Light</h2>

<p>The process for turning off the tire maintenance light is relatively simple. The first step is to check the tire pressure of each tire on your Nissan Murano. To do this, you’ll need a tire pressure gauge. Once you’ve checked the tire pressure, you can adjust it to the recommended pressure for your vehicle. </p>

<p>Once you’ve adjusted the tire pressure, you can turn off the tire maintenance light. To do this, you’ll need to press and hold the “reset” button located on the dashboard of your Nissan Murano. Hold the button until the light turns off. </p>
