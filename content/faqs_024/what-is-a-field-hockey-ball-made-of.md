<h2>What Is Field Hockey?</h2>

Field hockey is a team sport in which players use sticks to hit a ball into the opposing team’s goal. The game is played on a rectangular field, usually made of grass or turf, with a goal at each end. The game is split into two halves, with teams alternating between offense and defense. The team with the most goals at the end of the game is the winner. Field hockey is an intense and exciting game, and it requires a variety of specialized equipment in order for it to be played properly.
