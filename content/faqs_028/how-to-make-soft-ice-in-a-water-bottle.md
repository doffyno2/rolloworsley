<p>Making soft ice in a water bottle is an easy and fun way to enjoy a cool treat on a hot summer day. Soft ice is a type of frozen treat that is made by freezing a flavored liquid into small ice cubes. This type of ice is softer than regular ice cubes and is perfect for adding to cool beverages or eating on its own. Here are the steps to making soft ice in a water bottle.</p>

<h2>Ingredients You Will Need</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Flavored juice or beverage (such as Kool-Aid)</li>
	<li>Sugar (optional)</li>
	<li>Plastic water bottle</li>
</ul>

<h2>Steps to Make Soft Ice</h2>

<ol>
	<li>Pour the desired amount of flavored juice or beverage into the plastic water bottle.</li>
	<li>Add sugar to the juice or beverage if desired.</li>
	<li>Place the lid on the water bottle and shake it to mix the ingredients.</li>
	<li>Place the water bottle in the freezer and let it sit for at least four hours.</li>
	<li>Remove the water bottle from the freezer and carefully open the lid.</li>
	<li>Tip the water bottle over and let the soft ice cubes slide out into a bowl.</li>
	<li>Enjoy the soft ice cubes as is or add them to a cool beverage.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Tips for Making Soft Ice</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Do not fill the water bottle too full as the liquid will expand when frozen.</li>
	<li>If the soft ice cubes are too hard, return them to the freezer for an additional hour.</li>
	<li>For a more flavorful soft ice, use a flavored juice or beverage with more sugar.</li>
	<li>For a more colorful soft ice, add a few drops of food coloring to the juice or beverage.</li>
</ul>

<p>You should freeze the water bottle for at least four hours. If the soft ice cubes are too hard, return them to the freezer for an additional hour.</p>
