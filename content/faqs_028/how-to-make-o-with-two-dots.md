<h2>Step 1: Get the Right Materials</h2>

<p>The first step in making an “O” with two dots is to get the right materials. You’ll need two dots, a ruler, and a pencil. The dots should be approximately the same size. If you’re using paper dots, they should be between 1/2 inch and 1 inch in diameter.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Draw the Lines</h2>

<p>Once you have the right materials, it’s time to start drawing. Using the ruler and pencil, draw a line connecting the two dots. Make sure the line is straight and centered between the two dots. You may want to take a few practice runs before drawing the final line.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Connect the Dots</h2>

<p>Once the line is drawn, it’s time to connect the dots. Using the same pencil, draw a line from one dot to the other, going around the line you just drew. Make sure the line is centered between the two dots and that it forms a perfect “O” shape. If you’re not happy with the first attempt, take a few practice runs before drawing the final line.</p>
