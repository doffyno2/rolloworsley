<h2>What is a Pinion Seal?</h2>

<p>A pinion seal is a component of the drive axle that is responsible for preventing oil from leaking out of the differential. The pinion seal is made up of a metal outer shell with a rubber seal on the inside. The pinion seal is responsible for keeping the lubricant in the differential and preventing it from leaking out.</p>

<h2>What Causes a Pinion Seal Leak?</h2>

<p>Pinion seal leaks can be caused by a number of factors, including improper installation, inadequate lubrication, wear and tear and age. Improper installation or inadequate lubrication can cause the pinion seal to wear out prematurely, resulting in a leak. In addition, wear and tear over time can cause the pinion seal to deteriorate, resulting in a leak.</p>
