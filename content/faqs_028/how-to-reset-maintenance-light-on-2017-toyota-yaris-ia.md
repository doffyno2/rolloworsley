<h2>Step 1: Locate the OBD-II Port</h2> 

<p>The first step to resetting the maintenance light on the 2017 Toyota Yaris IA is to locate the OBD-II port. This port is usually located underneath the dashboard on the driver's side of the vehicle. Once you have located the port, plug in your OBD-II scanner.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Connect the Scanner to the Vehicle</h2>

<p>Once you have plugged in your OBD-II scanner, you will need to connect it to the vehicle. To do this, simply follow the instructions on the scanner's display. Once the scanner has been connected to the vehicle, you will be ready to reset the maintenance light.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Select the “Maintenance Light Reset” Option</h2>

<p>The next step is to select the “Maintenance Light Reset” option on the scanner's display. This will allow you to reset the maintenance light on your 2017 Toyota Yaris IA. Once you have selected this option, the scanner will begin the reset process.</p>

<p>The last step is to follow the on-screen instructions. The scanner will provide detailed instructions on how to reset the maintenance light on your 2017 Toyota Yaris IA. Simply follow the instructions and your maintenance light will be successfully reset.</p>
