<p>The maintenance light is an indicator that your Honda CRV needs service. <h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2002 Honda CRV</h2>

<p>Once you’ve taken your Honda CRV in for the required maintenance, you’ll need to reset the maintenance light. This can be done in just a few easy steps. First, turn off the engine and make sure the headlights are off. Then, insert the key into the ignition and turn it to the “on” position. You should see a dashboard light that looks like an oil can with a wrench inside. Press and hold the odometer reset button for five seconds. The maintenance light should now be reset.</p>
