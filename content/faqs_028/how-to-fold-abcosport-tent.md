<p>Before folding the tent, you'll need to remove any poles, stakes, and other items that are attached to it. Make sure you keep all of the parts together and store them in a safe place. <h2>Step 2: Fold the Tent in Half</h2>

<p>Once all gear has been removed, you can begin to fold the tent in half. Start at the center of the tent and fold it in half lengthwise. Then fold the tent in half again, this time width-wise. This will create a neat, rectangular shape for easy storage.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Roll the Tent Up</h2>

<p>With the tent folded in half, roll it up from the center outwards. Make sure to roll it as tight as possible to reduce the amount of space it will take up when stored. <p>Once the tent is rolled up, secure it with a bungee cord or a sturdy piece of rope. This will ensure that the tent stays rolled up and in good condition while stored. You can also use some of the gear that came with the tent, such as the poles, stakes, and other items, to secure the tent.</p>
