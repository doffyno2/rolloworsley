<p>The first step in cutting soccer socks is to measure them for the correct length. You will want to measure from the heel of the foot to about two inches above the ankle. <h2>Cutting the Soccer Socks</h2>
<p>Once you have your scissors ready, it is time to begin cutting the soccer socks. Start by placing the socks flat on a table, with the heel of the foot facing up.
