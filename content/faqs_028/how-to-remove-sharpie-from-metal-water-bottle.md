<p>It can be really annoying when you accidentally get permanent marker on your metal water bottle. But don’t worry, it doesn’t have to stay there forever. There are several ways to remove Sharpie from a metal water bottle that don’t require a lot of effort and can be done in just a few minutes.</p>

<h2>Method 1: Rubbing Alcohol</h2>

<p>Rubbing alcohol is one of the best and most effective ways to remove Sharpie from a metal water bottle. Before you get started, make sure the metal water bottle is washed and completely dry. Place a small amount of rubbing alcohol on a cotton ball or soft cloth and gently rub the Sharpie stain. Continue rubbing the stain until it has been completely removed. Once you’re finished, rinse the metal water bottle with warm water and let it air dry.</p>

<h2>Method 2: Hand Sanitizer</h2>

<p>Hand sanitizer can also be used to remove Sharpie from a metal water bottle. Take a small amount of hand sanitizer and rub it onto the area with the Sharpie stain. Allow it to sit for a few minutes and then scrub the area with a soft cloth or cotton ball. Rinse the metal water bottle with warm water and let it air dry.</p>

<h2>Method 3: Nail Polish Remover</h2>

<p>Nail polish remover is also a great way to remove permanent marker from a metal water bottle. Apply a small amount of nail polish remover to a cotton ball or soft cloth and gently rub the Sharpie stain. Allow the nail polish remover to sit for a few minutes before scrubbing the area. Once the stain has been removed, rinse the metal water bottle with warm water and let it air dry.</p>
