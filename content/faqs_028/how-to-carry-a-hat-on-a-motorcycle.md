<h2>Secure Your Hat to Your Body</h2>

<p>The most secure way to carry a hat on a motorcycle is to secure it to your body. You can do this by using a helmet clip or by tying a piece of string or elastic around your hat and attaching it to your clothing. If you have a full-face helmet, you can also attach your hat to the chin strap. Just make sure that whatever you use to secure the hat is strong enough to hold it in place, but not so tight that it restricts your movement or causes discomfort. You can also purchase special headgear designed specifically for motorcycle riding.</p>
