<h2>What is the Oil Maintenance Light?</h2>

<p>The oil maintenance light, sometimes referred to as the "oil change" light, is a warning light located on the dashboard of your Lexus IS250. It is designed to let you know when the engine oil needs to be changed. The light is usually a yellow or orange color, and it will come on when the oil has reached a certain level of wear and tear. It is important to not ignore this light and to change the oil as soon as possible. If the oil is not changed, it can lead to engine problems and reduced performance.</p>

<h2>How to Reset the Oil Maintenance Light</h2>

<p>The first step in resetting the oil maintenance light is to make sure that you have changed the oil. If you haven't already done so, it is important to do this before resetting the light. After the oil has been changed, the light can be reset in just a few simple steps:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Turn the ignition on, but don't start the engine.</li>
  <li>Press and hold the trip odometer reset button for about 10 seconds.</li>
  <li>Release the button and the oil maintenance light should go off.</li>
</ul>
