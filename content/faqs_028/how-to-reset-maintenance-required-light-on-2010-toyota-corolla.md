Resetting the maintenance required light is a simple process, but it’s important to do it correctly. Read on to learn how to reset the maintenance required light on your 2010 Toyota Corolla.</p>

<h2>Steps to Reset Maintenance Required Light on 2010 Toyota Corolla</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Turn off the ignition.</h3>

<p>The first step in resetting the maintenance required light is to turn off the ignition. This can be done by turning the key in the ignition to the off position.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Push and hold the odometer reset button.</h3>

<p>Once the ignition is off, locate the odometer reset button, which is located just to the left of the steering wheel, on the instrument panel. Push and hold the reset button for 10 seconds.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Release the odometer reset button.</h3>

<p>Once you’ve held the button for 10 seconds, release it. The maintenance required light should now be reset and the vehicle should be ready for its next service.</p>
