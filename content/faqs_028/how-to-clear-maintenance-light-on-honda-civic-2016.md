<h2>What is the Maintenance Light? </h2>

<p>The maintenance light on the Honda Civic 2016 is an indicator that alerts the driver when the car needs to be serviced. <h3>Step 2: Check the Owner's Manual </h3>

<p>The next step is to consult the owner's manual of the Honda Civic 2016. It will provide detailed instructions on how to clear the maintenance light. Generally, the process involves pressing and holding the maintenance light button for a few seconds until the light turns off.</p>
