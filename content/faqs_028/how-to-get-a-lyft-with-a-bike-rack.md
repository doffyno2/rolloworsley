<p>Getting a Lyft ride with a bike rack can be daunting if you’re not familiar with the process. But with a few simple steps you’ll be able to get a ride with your bike in tow. Here’s how to get a Lyft with a bike rack.</p>

<h2>Step 1: Download the Lyft App</h2>

<p>The first step in getting a Lyft ride with a bike rack is to download the Lyft app. You can find the app in the App Store for iOS devices or the Google Play Store for Android devices. Once you have the app downloaded, you’ll need to create an account and enter your payment information.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Request a Ride</h2>

<p>Once you have the app downloaded and your account set up, you can request a ride. When you request a ride, you’ll have the option to “add special requests.” This is where you’ll select the option for a bike rack. If a driver with a bike rack is available, they’ll be able to accept your ride request. You’ll be able to see their vehicle type, including whether or not it has a bike rack.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Load Your Bike</h2>

<p>Once the driver arrives, they’ll be able to help you load your bike onto the bike rack. Depending on the type of bike rack, you may need to remove the front wheel or use a bungee cord to secure the bike in place. Make sure your bike is secure before you get in the car.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Enjoy Your Ride</h2>

<p>Once your bike is loaded and secured, you can get in the car and enjoy your ride. The driver will be able to take you to your destination and make sure your bike is safely unloaded at the end of the ride.</p>
