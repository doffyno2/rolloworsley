<p>Google News is an online news aggregator provided by Google, presenting summaries of articles from various sources. <h2>Disable Google News in Device Settings</h2>

<p>The quickest and easiest way to get rid of Google News on your Android device is to disable it in the device’s settings. To do this, open the ‘Settings’ menu on your device, scroll down to the ‘Apps’ section, select ‘Google News’ and then tap on the ‘Disable’ option. This will remove the Google News app from your device, meaning you will no longer see any newsfeeds.</p>

<h2>Remove Google News from Home Screen</h2>

<p>If you don’t want to disable the app, you can simply remove it from the home screen. To do this, tap and hold the Google News icon on the home screen for a few seconds, then select ‘Remove’. This will remove the icon from the home screen, but will still keep the app installed on your device.</p>

<h2>Uninstall Google News from Device</h2>

<p>If you want to completely get rid of Google News from your device, you can uninstall the app. To do this, open the ‘Settings’ menu on your device, select ‘Apps’, select ‘Google News’ and then tap on the ‘Uninstall’ option. This will completely remove the Google News app from your device, meaning you will no longer see any newsfeeds.</p>
