<p>To reset the maintenance light on a 2013 Prius C, first turn the ignition to the "on" position, but do not start the car. Next, press and hold the "trip" button on the dashboard. While continuing to hold the "trip" button, press and release the "reset" button located below it. Release the "trip" button and the maintenance light should reset.</p>

<p>If the maintenance light does not reset, it may be necessary to take the car to a dealership or qualified mechanic to diagnose and repair any underlying issues with the car's emissions system.</p>
