<h2>What is Origami?</h2>

Origami is the Japanese art of paper folding. It is a creative and fun way to make pieces of art out of paper. Origami is also used for decoration and can be used to make sculptures, jewelry, cards, and more. It is an enjoyable activity that does not require a lot of supplies or skill.

<h2>How to Make Origami Dog Money</h2>

Making origami dog money is an easy and fun craft that can be done by anyone. To make origami dog money, you will need paper, scissors, and a marker.

<h3>Step 1: Cut the Paper</h3>

Take a piece of paper and cut it into a square. Make sure the paper is not too thick or it will be difficult to fold.

<h3>Step 2: Draw the Dog Shape</h3>

Draw the outline of a dog on one side of the square. Be sure to make the shape as symmetrical as possible.

<h3>Step 3: Fold the Paper</h3>

Fold the paper in half along the center line of the dog shape. Unfold the paper and fold it in half again, but this time along the other center line of the dog shape.

<h3>Step 4: Create the Dog Body</h3>

Starting at the top of the dog shape, start folding the paper along the lines of the dog shape. Begin at the top and work your way down. Once you reach the bottom, tuck the edges of the paper into the body of the dog.

<h3>Step 5: Finish the Dog Money</h3>

Once the body of the dog is folded, you can use a marker to draw a face on the dog. You can also use other materials such as buttons or beads to give the dog more personality.
