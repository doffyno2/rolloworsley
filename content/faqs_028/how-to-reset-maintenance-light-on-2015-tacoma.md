<p>If you own a 2015 Toyota Tacoma, you will be familiar with the maintenance light. This light is a reminder from your truck that regular maintenance is due to keep it running at its best. <h2>Step 1: Locate the Maintenance Light on the Dashboard</h2>

<p>The first step is to find the maintenance light on the dashboard. You’ll want to look for a small light near the speedometer or tachometer. It may be labeled as a “Maintenance Light” or “Check Engine Light.” If you can’t find it, consult your owner’s manual for help.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Locate the Reset Button</h2>

<p>Once you’ve found the maintenance light, you’ll need to locate the reset button. This button is usually located underneath the dashboard near the steering wheel. There may be a few buttons, so make sure you press the one labeled “Maintenance Light Reset” or “Check Engine Light Reset.”</p>

<h2>Step 3: Reset the Maintenance Light</h2>

<p>Once you’ve located the reset button, simply press it. This should reset the maintenance light and turn it off. If the light does not turn off, try pressing the button again or consult your owner’s manual for further instructions.</p>
