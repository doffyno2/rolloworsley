<h2>What is the Maintenance Light?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light, or service light, is a reminder to the driver that the vehicle is due for a scheduled service. This light is typically located on the dashboard, and will come on when the vehicle's computer detects that it is time for a service. The maintenance light can be reset to turn it off, and this is what we will be discussing in this article.</p>
 
<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2017 Mazda CX-5</h2>

<p>In order to reset the maintenance light on a 2017 Mazda CX-5, you will need to do the following:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Turn the ignition to the “On” position.</li>
  <li>Press and hold the “Trip” button (located on the instrument panel) for approximately 5 seconds.</li>
  <li>Release the “Trip” button.</li>
  <li>The maintenance light should now be reset.</li>
</ul>

<p>It is important to note that resetting the maintenance light will not reset any other issues that may be present with the vehicle. It is also important to note that the maintenance light will come back on after a certain amount of time has passed.</p>
