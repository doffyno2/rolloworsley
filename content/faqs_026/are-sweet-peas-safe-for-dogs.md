<h2>What Are Sweet Peas?</h2>

Sweet peas are a type of legume that is commonly eaten as a vegetable. They are high in fiber, protein, and vitamins, making them a nutritious and delicious addition to your pet's diet. Sweet peas are also rich in antioxidants, which can help protect your pup from free radicals and other environmental toxins.

<h2>Are Sweet Peas Safe For Dogs?</h2>

Yes, sweet peas are generally considered safe for dogs. However, it is important to pay close attention to the type of sweet peas you are feeding your pup.
