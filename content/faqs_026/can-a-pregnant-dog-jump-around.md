<h2>The Benefits of Exercise During Pregnancy</h2>

Exercise is beneficial for both pregnant dogs and their puppies. It can help promote muscle and bone development, increase blood flow, and improve cardiovascular health. Exercise also helps to reduce stress, which can be beneficial for both the mother and her puppies.

<h2>When to Restrict Exercise</h2>

As the pregnancy progresses, the risk of physical damage to the puppies increases. During the second half of the pregnancy, it's important to restrict the dog's activities to prevent any damage to the puppies. This includes jumping, running, and other activities that put stress on the abdomen.
