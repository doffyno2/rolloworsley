<h2>How to Save Searches on eBay?</h2>

<p>Saving searches on eBay is easy and straightforward. All you need to do is sign in to your account, type your search query into the search bar, and then click the “Save this Search” button. Once you’ve saved a search, you can access it from the “My Saved Searches” page. </p>

<p>To save a search on eBay, follow these steps:  </p>

<ul>

<li>Click the “Save this Search” button.</li>
<li>Your search will now be saved to your “My Saved Searches” page.</li>
</ul>
