<h2>What Is an RV Inverter?</h2>
An RV inverter is an electrical device that converts the direct current (DC) power from your RV’s batteries into alternating current (AC) power. The AC power is then used to power your RV’s appliances, electronics and other devices.

<h2>How Does an RV Inverter Work?</h2>
An RV inverter works by converting the DC power stored in your RV’s batteries into AC power. The inverter takes the DC power from the batteries and converts it into AC power, which is then sent to your RV’s devices and appliances. The inverter also works in reverse, taking the AC power from your RV’s devices and appliances and converting it back into DC power, which is then stored in the batteries.
