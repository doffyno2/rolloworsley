<h2>What Are Hot Dogs?</h2>

Hot dogs are a type of processed sausage that is usually made from a combination of beef, pork, or chicken and spices. Hot dogs are a popular food choice for humans, and they can be found in many places, from grocery stores to fast-food restaurants.
