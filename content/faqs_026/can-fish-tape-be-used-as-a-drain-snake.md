<h2>What is a Fish Tape?</h2>

Fish tape, also known as an electrical fish tape, is a long, thin, steel tape that is used to route electrical wiring through walls or conduit. Fish tape is often used in residential and commercial installations to add new wiring or to repair existing wiring.

<h2>What is a Drain Snake?</h2>

A drain snake is a device used to clear clogs from a drain. It consists of a long, flexible cable with a cutting head that is inserted into the drainpipe in order to cut through the clog. The cable is then retrieved and the clog can be removed. Drain snakes come in a variety of sizes and shapes and are used for both residential and commercial applications.
