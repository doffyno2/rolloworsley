<h2>Why Fit Matters</h2>

When it comes to dirt biking, fit matters. If your pants are too loose, they can get in the way while you’re riding and can even be dangerous. If they’re too tight, they’ll be uncomfortable and restrictive. That’s why it’s important to find the right fit. 

<h2>Finding Your Size</h2>

When it comes to finding the right size of dirt bike pants, you need to know your measurements. You will need to measure the circumference of your waist, hips, and inseam. With these measurements in hand, you can then compare them to the sizing chart of the pants you’re interested in.
