<p>Rawhides are a popular treat for dogs, but they can also be a potential hazard. The main concern about rawhides is that they can go bad and potentially cause your dog to get sick. So, do dog rawhides go bad? The answer is yes, and it’s important to know the signs of spoilage. </p>

<h2>What Are Dog Rawhides?</h2>

<p>Dog rawhide are treats made from the hides of cows, horses, or other animals. The hides are cleaned and processed, then cut into chewable pieces. Rawhides are popular with dog owners because they are relatively inexpensive and long-lasting treats. They are also good for your dog’s dental health, since they help to scrape away plaque and tartar during chewing. </p>

<h2>Do Dog Rawhides Go Bad?</h2>

<p>Yes, rawhides can go bad. The pieces are cured with salt and other chemicals to help preserve them, but they do eventually spoil. The signs of spoilage can vary, but generally include a foul odor, discoloration, mold, or a slimy texture. Spoiled rawhides can be a health hazard for your dog, so it’s important to inspect them regularly and discard any that look or smell bad. </p>

<h2>How Long Do Dog Rawhides Last?</h2>

<p>It’s difficult to say exactly how long rawhides will last before they start to spoil. Generally speaking, rawhides can last anywhere from two to six months. However, this can vary depending on the type of rawhide, the size of the pieces, and how they are stored, so it’s important to inspect them regularly. </p>

<h2>How to Store Dog Rawhides?</h2>

<p>Proper storage is key to keeping rawhides fresh and safe for your dog. The most important thing to do is to keep them away from moisture. If possible, store rawhides in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. If you don’t have an airtight container, you can wrap the pieces in plastic wrap or aluminum foil. </p>

<h2>How to Tell if a Dog Rawhide is Spoiled?</h2>

<p>The best way to tell if a rawhide is spoiled is to inspect it closely. Spoiled rawhides will often have a foul odor, discoloration, or a slimy texture. If you notice any of these signs, discard the piece immediately. </p>

<h2>Can Spoiled Dog Rawhides Make My Dog Sick?</h2>

<p>Yes, spoiled rawhides can make your dog sick. Bacteria can grow on the pieces, leading to an upset stomach or other health problems. If your dog eats a spoiled rawhide, it’s important to keep an eye on them for signs of illness and contact your veterinarian if needed. </p>
