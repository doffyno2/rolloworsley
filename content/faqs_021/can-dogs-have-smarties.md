Can Dogs Have Smarties? 


It’s a question that many pet owners ask: can dogs have Smarties? While Smarties are generally seen as a harmless treat for kids, it’s important to understand that they contain ingredients that can be harmful to dogs. In this article, we’ll explore the potential risks posed by Smarties to your canine companion and provide advice on how to keep your dog safe.

<h2>Are Smarties Safe for Dogs?</h2>

The short answer is no, Smarties are not safe for dogs. The main ingredients of Smarties are sugar, cornstarch, and food coloring. These ingredients are not toxic to dogs, but they do provide a large amount of empty calories that can lead to weight gain, diabetes, and other health issues. In addition, the food coloring used in Smarties can cause an upset stomach and other gastrointestinal problems in dogs.

<h2>How Can I Keep My Dog Safe From Smarties?</h2>
