<h2>Signs of Pregnancy in Weenie Dogs</h2>

There are a few signs that may indicate that a weenie dog is pregnant. These signs include:

<h3>Visible Abdominal Swelling</h3>

One of the most common signs of pregnancy in weenie dogs is visible abdominal swelling. This swelling is caused by the growing puppies inside the mother's womb.

<h3>Increased Appetite</h3>

Weenie dogs that are pregnant often have an increased appetite. This is due to the fact that they need more calories to support the growing puppies.

<h3>Weight Gain</h3>

Pregnant weenie dogs typically gain weight as their puppies grow inside the womb. This is due to the increased caloric intake that is needed to support the puppies.

<h3>Changes in Behavior</h3>

Pregnant weenie dogs may also exhibit changes in behavior. This can include increased sleepiness, irritability, and nesting behaviors.
