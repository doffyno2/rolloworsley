<h2>Log Out of Mail on iPhone</h2>

<p>Logging out of the Mail app on your iPhone is easy and only takes a few steps. To get started, open the Mail app on your iPhone and tap on the profile icon in the upper right corner. <p>Once you have logged out of the Mail app, you will be brought back to the main page of the Mail app and you will no longer be logged in. You can now log in with a different account or share your device with someone else without worrying about them seeing any of your emails.</p>
