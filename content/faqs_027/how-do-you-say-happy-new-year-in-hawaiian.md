<h2>How to Say “Happy New Year” in Hawaiian</h2>

<p>Now that you know a little bit about the Hawaiian language, let’s get to the fun part: learning how to say “Happy New Year” in Hawaiian. The phrase you’ll be looking for is “Hau’oli Makahiki Hou”, which literally translates to “Happy New Year”. To make sure you pronounce it correctly, break it down into its individual components:</p>

<ul>
