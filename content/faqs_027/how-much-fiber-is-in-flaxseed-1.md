<h2>What Is Fiber?</h2>

<p>Fiber is a type of carbohydrate that your body can't digest. It passes through your digestive system undigested, which means that it provides your body with very few calories. Fiber is found in a wide variety of plant-based foods, including fruits, vegetables, beans, nuts, and whole grains.</p>
