<h2>What Is Rum?</h2>

Rum is a spirit made from fermented and distilled sugar cane juice or molasses. It’s usually aged in oak barrels, which gives it its distinctive flavor and dark color. Rum is commonly used in cocktails and can also be enjoyed on its own, either neat or over ice.

Spiced rum is flavored with spices and herbs to give it a unique flavor. Finally, flavored rum is infused with a variety of fruits or other flavors.
