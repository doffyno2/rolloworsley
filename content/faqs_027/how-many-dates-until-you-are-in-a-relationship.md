<h2>What Is a Relationship?</h2>

A relationship is a commitment between two people that involves trust, loyalty, and mutual respect. It can be a romantic relationship, such as marriage or a casual relationship, such as a friends-with-benefits arrangement. In any case, relationships involve both parties making an effort to make the other person happy and to meet their needs.
