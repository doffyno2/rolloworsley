<h2>Repairing an iPhoto Library</h2>

Once you've identified the cause of the problem, you can begin the process of repairing your iPhoto library. Depending on the cause, the repair process can vary, but here are some of the most common steps:

<h3>Rebuild the Library</h3>

Once you've recovered the data, the next step is to rebuild the library. This involves importing the recovered files into a new library, which can be done using iPhoto's Rebuild Library tool.
