<h2>What Is a Chipped Windshield?</h2>

<p>A chipped windshield is a crack or hole in the windshield that is usually caused by a flying object like a stone or pebble. This type of damage is usually quite small, but it can still adversely affect visibility and the overall integrity of the windshield. The size and shape of the chip will determine the best method for repair.</p>

<h2>How Much Does Chipped Windshield Repair Cost?</h2>

<p>The cost of repairing a chipped windshield will depend on several factors, including the size and location of the chip and the type of repair method used. Generally speaking, a basic chip repair can range from $50 to $150. For more complex or larger chips, the cost may be higher. It’s also important to note that some insurance companies may cover the cost of repairs, so it’s always a good idea to check with your provider before proceeding with any repairs.</p>

<h2>Types of Windshield Repair</h2>

<p>There are two main types of windshield repair: simple chip repair and glass replacement. Simple chip repair is generally used for smaller chips that are less than the size of a quarter. This type of repair typically involves filling the chip with a resin that is designed to bond with the glass and restore its structural integrity. On the other hand, glass replacement is used for larger chips and cracks, and involves completely replacing the damaged glass with a new piece. This type of repair is more expensive than chip repair, but it is often the only option for larger chips and cracks.</p>
