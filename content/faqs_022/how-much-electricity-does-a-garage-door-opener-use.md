<h2>How Does a Garage Door Opener Work?</h2>

A garage door opener is an electronic device that is used to open and close a garage door. It works by sending a signal to a motor that is connected to the door, which then moves the door up or down. The opener can be operated manually or with a remote control.

<h2>What Type of Electricity Does a Garage Door Opener Use?</h2>

The type of electricity that a garage door opener uses depends on the type of opener you have. Most openers use either AC (alternating current) or DC (direct current) power. AC power is typically used for older openers, while DC power is more common in newer models.

<h2>How Much Electricity Does a Garage Door Opener Use?</h2>
