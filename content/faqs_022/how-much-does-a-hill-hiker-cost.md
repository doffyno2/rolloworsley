<h2>What is the Cost of Hiring a Guide?</h2>

The cost of hiring a guide will depend on the length of the hike, the difficulty of the terrain, and the guide's experience and qualifications. On average, a guide can cost between £50 and £100 per day, depending on the experience, qualifications, and the length of the hike. It is also important to factor in any additional costs such as travel and accommodation, which can add up to a significant amount.

<h2>What Equipment and Clothing Do You Need?</h2>

The cost of hill walking equipment and clothing can vary significantly depending on the type of gear you need and the quality of the items you buy. It is important to purchase items of the highest quality, as the cost of replacing them in the future could be high.
