<h2>Domestic Flights</h2>

<p>If you're flying domestically, it's recommended that you arrive at Nashville Airport at least two hours before your scheduled departure time. This allows for enough time to check-in, go through security, and make it to your gate with plenty of time to spare. If you're flying during peak travel periods, you may want to arrive even earlier to beat the crowds. Additionally, if you're flying with a budget airline such as Southwest or Frontier, you may have to check-in at the ticket counter rather than online, so be sure to factor that into your arrival time.</p>

<h2>International Flights</h2>
