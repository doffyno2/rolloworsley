<h2>What is Mountain Climbing?</h2>

Mountain climbing is a form of mountaineering, which is the art and science of climbing mountains. The goal of mountain climbing is to reach the summit of a mountain or other large incline. Mountain climbers often use ropes, harnesses, and other safety equipment to ensure their safety during the climb.
