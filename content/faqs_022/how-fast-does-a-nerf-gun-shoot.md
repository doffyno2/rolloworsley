Nerf guns use a variety of mechanisms to fire their darts. Depending on the type of Nerf gun, they may use a spring-loaded mechanism or a flywheel mechanism.

Flywheel Nerf guns use a motor to spin a wheel that propels the dart forward. This type of Nerf gun is more expensive and complex, but can fire darts at much faster speeds.
