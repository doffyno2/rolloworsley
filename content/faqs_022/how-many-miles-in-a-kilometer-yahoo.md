<h2>What is a Mile?</h2>

<p>A mile is a unit of length that is equal to 5,280 feet or 1.609 kilometers. Miles are often used for long distances, such as for measuring the distance between two cities. The term “mile” is derived from the Latin word “mille”, which means “thousand”.</p>

<h2>What is a Kilometer?</h2>

<p>A kilometer is a unit of length that is equal to 1,000 meters or 0.6214 miles. The term “kilometer” is derived from the Greek word “kilos”, which means “thousand”.</p>

<h2>How to Convert Miles to Kilometers</h2>

<p>To convert miles to kilometers, simply multiply the number of miles by 1.609. For example, if you have 10 miles, then 10 x 1.609 = 16.09 kilometers.</p>

<h2>How to Convert Kilometers to Miles</h2>

<p>To convert kilometers to miles, simply multiply the number of kilometers by 0.6214. For example, if you have 16.09 kilometers, then 16.09 x 0.6214 = 10 miles.</p>
