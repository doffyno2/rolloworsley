<p>Taking your pup for a week-long stay at a boarding facility can be expensive, but it can also give you peace of mind knowing your furry friend is in good hands. However, the cost of dog boarding for a week can vary greatly depending on the services and amenities offered. So, how much does dog boarding cost for a week?</p>

<p>The cost of dog boarding for a week depends on many factors, including the facility you choose, the level of care your pup will receive, and any special services or amenities you request. Some general kennel-style dog boarding facilities may charge as low as $25 to $50 a night, while high-end, full-service pet resorts can cost up to $100 or more per night. Additionally, some facilities may charge a flat rate for a week-long stay, while others may offer discounts for multiple nights.</p>

<p>For example, some kennels may charge $200 for a five-night stay, while a pet resort may charge $125 per night for a single dog in a standard suite. Additionally, some facilities may charge extra fees for add-on services such as grooming, training, or special dietary needs. It’s important to ask the facility in advance about their rates and any additional fees they may charge.</p>

<p>When considering the cost of dog boarding for a week, it’s important to consider the following factors:</p>

Additionally, some facilities may charge extra for pick-up and drop-off services if they are located a certain distance from the owner’s home.</p>

<h3>Level of Care</h3>

<p>The cost of dog boarding for a week can also vary depending on the level of care your pup will receive. <h3>Size of the Dog</h3>

<h3>Add-On Services</h3>
