<p>Typically, the cost of a washing machine motor can range from $50 to $500 or more. The most common type is a direct drive motor, which is the most cost-effective option and is suitable for many types of washing machines. </p>

<p>Induction motors are more expensive, but are quieter and more efficient. They are suitable for front-loading washing machines and some top-loading models. PSC motors are the most expensive, but they are quieter and more efficient than induction motors. They are typically used in high-end top-loading washing machines.</p>
