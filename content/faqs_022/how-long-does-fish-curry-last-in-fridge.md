<h2>How Long Does Fish Curry Last in the Fridge?</h2>

<p>In general, cooked fish curry will last in the fridge for 3-4 days. If stored properly, it can last up to 5 days. To extend the shelf life of fish curry, it is important to store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator. The airtight container will help to keep the fish curry fresh for the longest possible time.</p>

<h2>How to Store Fish Curry for the Longest Shelf Life</h2>

<p>It is important to store fish curry properly in order to extend its shelf life. The following tips will help you to store fish curry properly:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Store the fish curry in an airtight container. This will help to keep the food fresh and prevent bacteria from growing on the food.</li>
  <li>Place the airtight container in the refrigerator. The cold temperature of the refrigerator will help to slow down the growth of bacteria. </li>
  <li>If the airtight container is not enough to store the fish curry, you can also use a plastic bag to store it. It is important to ensure that the plastic bag is sealed tight so that no moisture can enter the bag.</li>
  <li>Label the container with the date of cooking. This will help you to easily identify the food and also help you to know when to throw it away.</li>
  <li>If you want to freeze the fish curry for longer shelf life, make sure to place the airtight container in the freezer. This will help to prevent ice crystals from forming on the food.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
