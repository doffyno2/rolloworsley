<p>A van is a type of motor vehicle typically used for transporting goods or passengers. They can range in size from small minivans to large full-size vans. <h2>How Many Seats Does a Typical Van Have?</h2>

<p>The number of seats in a van can vary significantly, depending on the make and model of the van.
