# How Long Does a NexGard Pill Last For Dogs?

NexGard is an oral flea and tick preventative medication specifically designed for dogs. It is available in beef-flavored chewable tablets that your pup will love. As a pet parent, you may be wondering just how long a NexGard pill will last for your canine companion. The answer is not as straightforward as you may think.

## What is NexGard?

NexGard is an oral flea and tick preventative for dogs that comes in the form of a chewable tablet. The active ingredient in NexGard is afoxolaner, an insecticide and acaricide that kills fleas and ticks. The medication starts to work within 24 hours of administration and provides protection for a full month.

## How Does NexGard Work?

NexGard works by killing fleas and ticks on contact. It is important to note that NexGard does not repel fleas and ticks, so it cannot prevent them from attaching to your pup. Instead, it kills fleas and ticks that are already present on your pup's body.

## How Long Does a NexGard Pill Last?

NexGard is an effective flea and tick preventative medication that will last for a full month after administration. This means that you should give your pup a new chewable tablet once a month to continue providing protection against fleas and ticks. However, it is important to note that the effectiveness of the medication may decrease if it is not given on a regular basis.

## Is NexGard Safe for Dogs?

NexGard is safe for dogs of all sizes. It is important to talk to your veterinarian prior to giving your pup NexGard to make sure that it is the right medication for your pup's needs. Your vet can also help you determine the proper dosage for your pup's weight and age.

## What Are the Side Effects of NexGard?

Like any medication, NexGard can cause side effects in some dogs. The most common side effects are vomiting, itching, and diarrhea. If your pup experiences any of these side effects, contact your vet right away.

## How Do I Give My Dog NexGard?

NexGard comes in the form of a beef-flavored chewable tablet that your pup will love. It is important that you give your pup the entire tablet once a month. If you are unsure of how to give your pup the medication, talk to your vet.

## How Do I Store NexGard?

NexGard should be stored at room temperature in a dry, dark place. It should not be stored in the refrigerator or in direct sunlight.
