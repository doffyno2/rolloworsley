<h2>Portland's Average Annual Sunshine</h2>

On average, Portland, Oregon, gets about 166 sunny days per year. That's an average of about 14 days per month. This makes Portland a great place to live if you're looking for sunny days and plenty of outdoor activities. 

<h2>Summer Sunshine in Portland</h2>

During the summer months, Portland is particularly sunny. June, July, and August are the months that get the most sunshine, with an average of about 22 days of sun each month. This makes Portland an ideal destination for anyone looking for some fun in the sun.
