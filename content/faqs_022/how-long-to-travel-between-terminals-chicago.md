<h2>Airport Shuttle Bus</h2>

<p>The quickest and most convenient way to get between terminals at O’Hare International Airport is to use the airport’s shuttle bus service. <p>If you’re traveling on a budget, public transportation is another option to get between terminals at O’Hare International Airport.
