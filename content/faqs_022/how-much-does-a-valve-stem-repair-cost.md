<h2>What is Valve Stem Repair?</h2>

Valve stem repair is a process of replacing or repairing the valves that control the flow of air and fuel into and out of the engine. The valve stem is a long skinny part that connects the cylinder head to the intake manifold or the exhaust manifold. It can become worn or damaged over time, leading to poor performance and higher fuel consumption.

<h2>How Much Does Valve Stem Repair Cost?</h2>

The cost of valve stem repair depends on the make and model of your car and the extent of the repair. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 to $1,000 for a valve stem repair. The cost of the job will also vary depending on the labor costs in your area.
