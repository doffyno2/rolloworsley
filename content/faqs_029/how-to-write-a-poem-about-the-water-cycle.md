<h2>Understanding the Water Cycle</h2>

<p>In order to write a good poem about the water cycle, it is important to first understand exactly what the water cycle is. The water cycle is the process by which water is circulated between the atmosphere, land and oceans. This process involves evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and infiltration. Evaporation occurs when water evaporates from the ocean, land and other bodies of water. Condensation occurs when the water vapor turns back into liquid water. Precipitation occurs when the condensation falls back to the ground as snow, rain, or hail. Finally, infiltration occurs when the precipitation seeps into the ground to replenish the groundwater supply.</p>
