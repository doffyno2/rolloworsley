<p>Bungee cords, also known as shock cords, are an essential part of any backpacker's gear. They are great for packing items securely and keeping them tightly in place, but they can also be used for a variety of other purposes. So, what items should go in bungee cords on a backpack?</p>

<h2>What are Bungee Cords?</h2>

<p>Bungee cords are elastic cords with hooks at either end. They come in many different sizes and lengths and are made from synthetic rubber, polypropylene, and polyester. The elasticity of bungee cords makes them very versatile, allowing them to be used for a variety of tasks, from securing items and keeping them in place to providing extra support for a variety of items. They are also relatively lightweight and easy to carry, making them a great item to have in a backpack.</p>

<h2>Uses for Bungee Cords</h2>

<p>Bungee cords can be used for a variety of tasks, including:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Securing items to the outside of a backpack or other bag</li>
  <li>Providing extra support for items such as tents or sleeping bags</li>
  <li>Tying down items such as canoes, kayaks, and other recreational equipment</li>
  <li>Providing an anchor point for attaching carabiners or other items</li>
  <li>Securely tying items together for storage</li>
</ul>

<h2>Items to Put in Bungee Cords</h2>

<p>Bungee cords can be used to secure a wide variety of items, including:</p>

  <li>Tents</li>
  <li>Food and water bottles</li>

  <li>Recreational equipment</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Secure Items with Bungee Cords</h2>

<p>Securing items with bungee cords is easy. Simply attach one end of the bungee cord to the item you want to secure and the other end to the outside of the backpack. Make sure that the cord is snug and secure, but not so tight that it causes damage to the item or the backpack. You can also use carabiners or other items to secure the bungee cord if needed.</p>

<h2>Tips for Using Bungee Cords</h2>

<p>When using bungee cords to secure items, there are a few tips to keep in mind:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Be careful not to over-tighten the bungee cord. It should be snug, but not so tight that it causes damage.</li>
  <li>Make sure that the hooks are securely attached to the backpack or other items.</li>
  <li>Check the bungee cord periodically to make sure it is still secure.</li>
  <li>Add extra carabiners or other items if needed to provide extra support and security.</li>
</ul>
