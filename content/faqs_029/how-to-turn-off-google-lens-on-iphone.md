<h2>How to Turn Off Google Lens on iPhone?</h2>

<p>If you don’t want to use Google Lens or you want to prevent it from using the camera of your device, you can easily turn it off. Here’s how:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Open the Google app on your iPhone.</li>
  <li>Tap the three-dot menu at the bottom-right corner of the screen.</li>
  <li>Tap Settings and then tap Google Lens.</li>
  <li>Tap the toggle next to the “Google Lens” option to turn it off.</li>
</ol>

<p>Once you have disabled the Google Lens feature, it will no longer use the camera of your device. You can also turn it off from the Settings app of your iPhone.</p>
