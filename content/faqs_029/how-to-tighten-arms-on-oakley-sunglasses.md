<h2>Why Adjust Oakley Sunglasses?</h2>

Adjusting the arms of your Oakley sunglasses is important for two reasons. Firstly, it allows for a more comfortable fit. <h2>How to Tighten Arms on Oakley Sunglasses</h2>

Tightening the arms of your Oakley sunglasses is a relatively simple process. You will need a pair of pliers, a screwdriver, and some patience. 

<h3>Step 1: Unscrew the Screws</h3>

The first step is to unscrew the screws that are located on the hinges of your Oakley sunglasses. These are usually located on the inside of the arms. Use a small screwdriver to loosen the screws. 

<h3>Step 2: Move the Arms</h3>

Once the screws are loosened, you can move the arms of your Oakley sunglasses. Gently pull the arms away from your face and adjust them to the desired position. 

<h3>Step 3: Tighten the Screws</h3>

Once the arms are in the correct position, use a pair of pliers to tighten the screws. Make sure that the screws are tight enough to keep the arms in place. 

<h3>Step 4: Test the Sunglasses</h3>

Finally, test the sunglasses to make sure that the arms are securely in place. Put the sunglasses on your face and make sure that they stay in place. If they slip down your nose, you may need to adjust the arms again.
