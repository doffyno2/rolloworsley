<p>The reset button is typically located near the hood release on the left side of the driver's seat. It is usually labeled "maintenance light reset" or something similar. Once you have located the reset button, you will need to press and hold it until the maintenance light turns off.</p>

<p>Once the maintenance light has turned off, you will need to turn the ignition off. Wait for a few seconds and then turn the ignition back on. This will ensure that the resetting process has been completed.</p>
