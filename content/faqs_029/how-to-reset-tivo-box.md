<h2>What Is a Tivo Box?</h2>

<p>A Tivo box is a device that connects to your television and allows you to watch your favorite shows, movies, and sports. When connected to your television, your Tivo box will allow you to record shows and play them back at any time. You can also pause and rewind live TV shows. </p>

<h2>Why Would You Need to Reset Your Tivo Box?</h2>

<p>Your Tivo box may need to be reset if it stops working properly. This can happen for a variety of reasons. It could be due to a power outage, or if you have recently changed your cable or satellite provider. It could also be due to a software update, or if you have recently purchased a new Tivo box. Resetting your Tivo box will often resolve any issues you may be having. </p>

<h2>How to Reset Your Tivo Box</h2>

<p>The process for resetting your Tivo box will vary depending on the model you have. Most Tivo boxes have a reset button on the back of the device. If your Tivo box has a reset button, press and hold it for 10 seconds. This will reset your Tivo box to its default settings. </p>

<p>If your Tivo box does not have a reset button, you will need to use the Tivo remote to reset your device. To do this, press the following buttons in sequence: Thumbs Up, Thumbs Down, Play, Rewind, Play, Fast Forward. This will reset your Tivo box to its default settings. </p>

<h2>What Will Happen When You Reset Your Tivo Box?</h2>

<p>When you reset your Tivo box, all of your settings, recordings, and recorded shows will be erased. The device will also be reset to its factory settings. You will need to set up your Tivo box again, including setting up your cable or satellite provider. </p>
