<p>The first thing you’ll need is a smoker. Fortunately, there are a variety of small smokers that are designed for camping. There are electric smokers, charcoal smokers, and even wood smokers. <p>Once you have all the equipment you need, you’ll need to prepare the tri tip. Start by trimming the fat off the meat. <h2>Lighting the Smoker</h2>

<p>Once the tri tip is prepared, it’s time to light the smoker. If you’re using a charcoal smoker, you’ll need to light the charcoal first. If you’re using an electric smoker, fill the water pan and plug it in. Once the smoker is lit, let it preheat for at least 15 minutes.</p>

<h2>Smoking the Tri Tip</h2>

<p>Once the smoker is preheated, it’s time to start smoking the tri tip. Place the tri tip on the grate and close the lid. Let the tri tip cook for about 1 hour. Remove the tri tip from the smoker and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.</p>
