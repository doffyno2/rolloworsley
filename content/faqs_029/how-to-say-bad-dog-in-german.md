<h2>The Basics of Saying “Bad Dog” in German</h2>

The simplest way to say “bad dog” in German is “schlechter Hund.” The literal translation of this phrase is “bad dog,” but it can also mean “naughty dog” or “badly behaved dog.” You can use this phrase to scold your pup in any situation where he or she has done something wrong.
