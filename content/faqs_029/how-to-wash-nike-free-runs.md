Nike Free Runs are some of the most popular shoes out there. They’re stylish and comfortable, but they’re also quite expensive. So when it comes to taking care of them, you want to make sure that you do it correctly. You don’t want to do anything that could potentially damage the shoes. Here’s a guide on how to wash Nike Free Runs so that your shoes look as good as new for a long time.

<h2>Things You Need to Wash Nike Free Runs</h2>

Before you start washing your Nike Free Runs, make sure that you have the following items:

<ul>
<li>A soft bristled brush</li>
<li>A mild detergent</li>
<li>A clean cloth or sponge</li>
<li>A soft cloth for polishing</li>
</ul>

<h2>Steps to Clean Nike Free Runs</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Prepare the Shoes</h3>

Before you start washing your Nike Free Runs, take them off and check them for any loose threads or dirt. If there are any, remove them carefully with your fingers or a soft brush.

<h3>Step 2: Prepare the Detergent</h3>

Mix the detergent with a small amount of warm water to create a foam. Make sure that the foam is not too thick or too thin.

<h3>Step 3: Start Cleaning</h3>

Once you have prepared the detergent, dip a soft cloth or sponge in it and start cleaning the shoes. Start from the top and work your way down. Make sure that you pay special attention to areas where the dirt accumulates such as the grooves and seams.

<h3>Step 4: Rinse the Shoes</h3>

Once you have finished cleaning, rinse the shoes with clean water. Make sure that you rinse off all the detergent from the shoes.

<h3>Step 5: Dry the Shoes</h3>

Once you have finished rinsing the shoes, let them air dry. Do not use a dryer or any other heat source as this may damage the shoes.

<h3>Step 6: Polish</h3>

Once the shoes are completely dry, you can apply a leather polish to give them extra shine and protection. Use a soft cloth to apply the polish and then buff the shoes with another soft cloth.

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How often should I clean my Nike Free Runs?</h3>

It is recommended that you clean your Nike Free Runs once every couple of weeks. This will help keep them looking like new for a longer period of time.

<h3>What kind of detergent should I use?</h3>

It is best to use a mild detergent when cleaning your Nike Free Runs. Avoid using harsh detergents as this may damage the shoes.

<h3>Can I use a dryer to dry my shoes?</h3>

No, it is not recommended that you use a dryer to dry your Nike Free Runs. The heat from the dryer may cause the shoes to shrink or crack.

<h3>Can I use a brush to clean my shoes?</h3>

Yes, it is okay to use a soft bristled brush to clean your Nike Free Runs. This will help get rid of any dirt or debris that is stuck in the grooves or seams.

<h3>Can I use any kind of polish?</h3>

It is best to use a leather polish when polishing your Nike Free Runs. Avoid using any other type of polish as this may damage the shoes.

<h3>Can I use a washing machine to clean my shoes?</h3>

No, it is not recommended that you use a washing machine to clean your Nike Free Runs. The agitation from the washing machine may damage the shoes.

<h3>Can I use a hairdryer to dry my shoes?</h3>

No, it is not recommended that you use a hairdryer to dry your Nike Free Runs. The heat from the hairdryer may cause the shoes to shrink or crack.
