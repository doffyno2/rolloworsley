<h2>How to Turn Off Your Garmin Watch</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Locate the Power Button</h3>

The power button is usually located on the top or side of the watch. Depending on the model of your watch, the power button may have a different design or location.

<h3>Step 2: Press and Hold the Power Button</h3>

Once you’ve located the power button, press and hold it for at least three seconds. This will cause the watch to enter sleep mode and shut down.

<h3>Step 3: Check the Screen</h3>

Once you’ve pressed and held the power button, the screen should go dark. This indicates that the watch has shut down.

<h3>Step 4: Charge the Watch</h3>

Once you’ve shut down the watch, it’s important to charge it up again. This will help ensure that the watch is ready to go when you need it next.
