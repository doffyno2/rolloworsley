<h2>What is Considered a Wide Calf?</h2>

<p>Generally, a wide calf is any circumference of the lower leg greater than 16 inches. However, this number can vary depending on the shoe manufacturer and brand. Depending on the brand, a wide calf may start at 17 inches, 18 inches, or even 19 inches. It is important to measure your calves before shopping for shoes to ensure a proper fit.</p>

<h2>How to Measure Your Calf</h2>

<p>Measuring your calf is easy. All you need is a soft measuring tape and a friend to help. To measure your calf, stand straight with your feet shoulder-width apart and have your friend measure the circumference of your calf. Measure the fullest part of your calf, about an inch below the knee. Once you have the measurement, compare it to the manufacturer’s specific sizing chart. This will help you determine what size of boot or shoe you need.</p>
