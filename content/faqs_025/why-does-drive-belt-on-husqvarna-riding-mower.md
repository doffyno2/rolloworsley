<h2>What Is a Drive Belt?</h2>

A drive belt is an important part of any Husqvarna riding mower. It is a flexible belt that is used to transfer power from the engine to the wheels. It is responsible for providing power to the wheels so that the mower can move. Without a drive belt, the wheels would not receive any power and the mower would not be able to move.

If the belt is not installed correctly, it can cause it to become misaligned and eventually break. Finally, the drive belt can break due to improper maintenance. If the belt is not lubricated regularly, it can cause the belt to wear out quickly and eventually break.
