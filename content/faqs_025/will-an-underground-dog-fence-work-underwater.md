<h2>What Is an Underground Dog Fence?</h2>

An underground dog fence is an electronic fence that encloses a property and keeps animals within a predetermined boundary. The fence uses a transmitter to create an invisible fence and a receiver to detect when your pet has crossed the boundary. If a pet does cross the boundary, a warning sound will emit from the receiver, prompting the pet to stay away from the boundaries.

<h2>What Are the Benefits of an Underground Dog Fence?</h2>

One of the main benefits of an underground dog fence is that it allows you to keep your pet safe without having to install a physical fence or a gate. It is also a cost-effective solution, as it requires minimal installation and maintenance costs. The system is also easy to use, as it can be easily installed and adjusted to fit your needs.

<h2>Will an Underground Dog Fence Work Underwater?</h2>

The short answer is no, an underground dog fence will not work underwater. This is because the transmitter used to create the invisible fence is not waterproof and would be damaged if submerged in water. Similarly, the receiver used to detect when a pet has crossed the boundary will also be damaged if submerged in water. 

<h2>Can I Install an Underground Dog Fence Near a Pool or Pond?</h2>

Although an underground dog fence will not work underwater, it can be installed near a pool or pond. However, you should keep in mind that the transmitter and receiver will need to be kept away from the water. This means that the fence should be installed at least four feet away from any body of water.
