<h2>What Causes Dogs to Eat Themselves to Death?</h2>

Dogs can develop compulsive eating disorders when they are stressed, bored, or anxious. This can lead to a dangerous cycle of overeating, which can eventually cause them to eat themselves to death. Other factors that can contribute to this behavior include:

<h3>Genetics</h3>

A dog's genetic makeup can play a role in their likelihood of developing compulsive eating habits. Dogs that have been bred to be especially hungry or to have a fast metabolism may be more prone to overeating.

<h3>Environment</h3>

The environment in which a dog is kept can also make them more likely to develop compulsive eating habits. Dogs that are kept in cramped, isolated spaces with limited food options may be more likely to overeat.

<h3>Health Problems</h3>

<h3>Mental Stimulation</h3>
