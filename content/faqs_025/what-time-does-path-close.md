<h2>Overview of PATH's Hours of Operation</h2>

The Port Authority Trans-Hudson Corporation, commonly known as PATH, is a rapid transit system connecting the cities of Newark, Hoboken, Jersey City, and Manhattan in the U.S. state of New Jersey. Operated by the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey, PATH provides 24-hour service on all its routes. However, depending on the day of the week and the time of day, the hours of operation may vary. 

<h2>PATH Operating Hours</h2>

On weekdays, PATH trains run from 5:00 am to 10:00 pm. On Saturdays and Sundays, the service begins at 6:00 am and ends at 11:00 pm. For holidays, the service hours are typically shortened. Additionally, the World Trade Center station is closed for overnight hours on Mondays through Fridays, from 11:00 pm to 5:00 am.
