What is the Average Arm Size?


When it comes to physical characteristics, our arms are one of the features that set us apart from one another. <h2>What Is the Average Arm Size for Men?</h2>

The average arm size for men is 14.2 inches, as measured around the largest circumference of the upper arm. This measurement is taken with the arm at rest, and does not include the hand or fingers. Men’s arms tend to be larger than women’s arms due to the higher levels of testosterone in men’s bodies, which causes them to have more muscle mass. 

<h2>What Is the Average Arm Size for Women?</h2>

The average arm size for women is 11.5 inches, as measured around the largest circumference of the upper arm.
