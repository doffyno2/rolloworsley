<h2>Why Wear Undergarments Under a Tennis Dress?</h2>
Undergarments play an important role in a tennis dress. Not only do they provide support and comfort, but they can also help regulate body temperature and keep you dry. The right undergarments can make a big difference in how comfortable you feel while playing and can help you stay focused on the game. 

<h2>Types of Undergarments to Wear Under a Tennis Dress</h2>
When choosing undergarments to wear under a tennis dress, there are several options to consider. The most common types of undergarments include sports bras, tank tops, shorts, and compression garments. 

<h3>Sports Bras</h3>
Sports bras are designed to provide support and prevent any discomfort or chafing that may occur during vigorous activity. They are usually made from moisture-wicking fabric and offer varying levels of compression. Sports bras are a great choice for those who need extra support or are playing in hot weather. 

<h3>Tank Tops</h3>
Tank tops are comfortable and lightweight pieces of clothing that can be worn under a tennis dress for extra coverage. They can also help keep you cool in hot weather and wick away sweat. Tank tops can be found in a variety of colors and styles, so you can find one that matches your style and needs. 

<h3>Shorts</h3>
Shorts are a great option for those who want to cover more of their legs while playing. They can provide extra coverage and can be made from a variety of materials, such as spandex, cotton, or nylon. Shorts can also help keep you cool in hot weather and provide some extra protection against abrasions. 

<h3>Compression Garments</h3>
Compression garments are designed to provide support and help regulate body temperature. They are usually made from moisture-wicking fabrics and offer varying levels of compression. Compression garments can help reduce fatigue and help you stay focused on the game.
